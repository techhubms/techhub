# The New Industrial Frontier: AI Collaboration and Copilot Agents in Manufacturing (BRK377)

## Overview

This session from Microsoft Ignite 2025 explores how industrial organizations can reimagine operations by merging human expertise with Microsoft AI technologies, such as Copilot agents and Azure IoT. The talk covers practical strategies to amplify worker productivity, ensure quality, and improve facility adaptability to meet modern challenges and accelerate innovation.

## Speakers

- Stephan Hotz
- Michael MacKenzie
- Rajesh Ramachandran (ABB)
- Tom Wisniewski

## Key Topics

- **Challenges of Remote Operations:** Examining the use of drones, robotics, and sensors to address remote and distributed industrial operations.
- **Azure IoT and AI Integration:** Demonstrations of leveraging Azure Internet of Things and Microsoft AI services to improve safety, efficiency, and enable autonomous operations.
- **Industrial AI Case Studies:** Insights from ABB on industrial deployment of AI, including agentic AI for managing large-scale solar plant operations.
- **Productivity and Automation Gains:** Showcasing AI-driven productivity improvements and advancements toward full autonomy in industrial settings.
- **Copilot Agents in Action:** Live demos of generative AI-powered support agents and AI device management, including day-in-the-life scenarios for frontline managers.
- **Democratizing Industrial Robotics:** Vision for making advanced robotics more accessible through modern software practices and AI integration.
