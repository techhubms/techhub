# How to understand any codebase in seconds

Onboarding to a new project can take hours of reading code. This video highlights a faster approach using **GitHub Copilot CLI**: you can ask for an overview, and Copilot will scan the repository and provide a **plain-English breakdown**.

## What this is demonstrating

- Use **GitHub Copilot CLI** to request a high-level overview of a repository
- Copilot scans the repo so you can avoid manually digging through folders
- The goal is quicker **codebase comprehension** and **faster onboarding**
