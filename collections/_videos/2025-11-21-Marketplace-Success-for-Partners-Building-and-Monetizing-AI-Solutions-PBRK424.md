# Marketplace Success for Partners: Building and Monetizing AI Solutions | PBRK424

**Session Overview**

In this Microsoft Ignite 2025 breakout, Microsoft experts share strategies for successfully leveraging the Microsoft Marketplace to build, launch, and monetize AI-powered solutions targeting both SMB and enterprise customers.

**Key Topics Covered:**

- The role of Microsoft Marketplace in the partner ecosystem
- Strategies for aligning your marketplace approach with sales and business growth
- Adapting to growing demand for intelligent applications and AI-driven agents
- Marketplace improvements and expanded adoption across large and small organizations
- How buying through Marketplace can help customers meet Microsoft commitments
- Success stories from real partners in the ecosystem
- Introduction of Arrow’s AI platform and the Our Sphere Assistant
- Announcement of Our Sphere AI 360 tool to help partners develop AI applications
- Insights on the future of AI partnerships, including Arrow’s AI Accelerator program
