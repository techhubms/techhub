# Demo: ice Contact Center Integrating Chatbots and Live Agents at Microsoft Ignite

**Speaker:** Mike Kraybill
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025
**Session ID:** ODSP1482

## Introduction

This session explores how the ice Contact Center platform enables organizations to manage customer interactions across live chat, voice, and SMS channels. The demonstration focuses on integrating a generative AI-powered chatbot (iceBot) for FAQ and automated support, followed by seamless escalation to a live agent for more complex queries.

## Key Capabilities

- **Real-Time Chatbot Responses:**
  - iceBot uses its knowledge base to provide immediate answers to typical customer questions.
  - Generative AI enhances the bot's ability to handle a wide variety of queries.
- **Multi-Channel Support:**
  - Similar automated support processes are available for voice and SMS.
- **Agent Handoff:**
  - When a question requires deeper insight, the chat is escalated to a live agent.
  - The agent joins the conversation for quick, personalized resolution.
- **Live Chat Features:**
  - iceChat delivers simple and effective text communication between customers and agents.
- **Post-Conversation Analytics:**
  - AI and translation services analyze conversations after completion, supporting feedback and quality improvement.

## Technical Highlights

- Integration of generative AI into FAQs and customer support workflows
- Smooth bot-to-agent transfer without loss of context
- Knowledge base retrieval via AI-powered search
- Support for multiple communication channels (chat, voice, SMS)
- Use of analytics and translation post-interaction
