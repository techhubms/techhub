# Accelerate Partner Innovation in Every Industry with Microsoft AI

## Session Overview

This Microsoft Ignite 2025 session brings together Microsoft leaders and partners to discuss driving industry innovation with AI. Kathleen Mitford introduces the concept of 'Frontier Transformation' and outlines Microsoft's FY26 Industry Strategy, focusing on empowering partners to deliver faster business value through AI engineering and integration.

### Key Topics

- **Applying AI Across Business:** How AI agents, ranging from simple to autonomous, are transforming different business functions.
- **Partner Collaboration:** Real-world case studies including Infosys and Microsoft building the 'Ask Ralph' virtual stylist for Ralph Lauren, as well as ServiceNow and Azureâ€™s joint implementations with clients like Lufthansa and Teleperformance.
- **Partner Designation & Skilling:** How Microsoft supports partner growth through designations, go-to-market (GTM) activities, and upskilling initiatives.
- **Product Roadmap Visibility:** Importance of transparency for partners in understanding Microsoft's product direction.
- **Showcases and Wrap-up:** Industry showcases of partner programs and ongoing appreciation for collaborative efforts.
