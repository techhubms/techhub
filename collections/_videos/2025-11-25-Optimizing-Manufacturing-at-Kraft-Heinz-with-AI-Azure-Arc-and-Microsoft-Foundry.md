# Optimizing Manufacturing at Kraft Heinz with AI, Azure Arc, and Microsoft Foundry

Microsoft Ignite 2025 features a customer-led session detailing Kraft Heinz’s journey through digital and AI transformation in manufacturing.

**Key Topics Covered:**

- The role of a digital core and digital twin technology in providing real-time operational guidance.
- Partnership with Microsoft beginning in 2023 to drive cross-functional AI innovation.
- Adoption of Azure Arc and Microsoft Foundry to scale AI capabilities across industrial environments.
- The digital twin maturity model and evolution toward autonomous manufacturing systems.
- Genuine autonomous material handling enabled by advanced AI and digital operations.
- Implementation of Connected Worker systems for operational digitization.
- Integration of 42 years of ketchup-making expertise using Copilot and digital solutions.

**Actionable Insights:**

- How to initiate a transformation roadmap leveraging Microsoft AI apps and agents.
- Scaling digital twin concepts to build intelligent manufacturing frameworks.
- Strategies for envisioning and achieving a self-driven, autonomous supply chain.
