# Partners Accelerate Media Innovation with AI Agents and Microsoft Cloud

## Overview

This session from Microsoft Ignite 2025 demonstrates how media organizations and partners are using Azure, Microsoft Fabric, and generative AI to drive faster, smarter content production and distribution, while creating richer and more immersive audience experiences.

## Key Topics

- **AI-First Frontier Firms & Leadership Readiness**
  - Introduction to media organizations leading the AI transformation
- **Real-World Examples**
  - Dentsu
  - La Liga
  - Le Monde
  - Hypercinema
- **Marketplace & Partner Growth**
  - Utilization of the Microsoft Marketplace to empower partners and fuel AI growth
- **Partner Success Stories**
  - NBA
  - Universal Studios
- **Guest Insights**
  - Tanya Khaiyanun (PwC)
  - Erik Ramberg (MediaKind)
- **MediaKind Perspectives**
  - Scaling live events and enhancing viewer engagement using AI
- **Redesigning Media Processes**
  - AI as a core business enabler for workflow automation and improved content delivery
- **Building Data Foundations**
  - Strategies for ensuring high data quality to maximize AI capabilities
- **Privacy & Intellectual Property**
  - Approaches to secure cloud architectures that address regulatory concerns

## Technical Focus

- Deployment and scaling of Azure and Fabric data platforms for media solutions
- Integration of generative AI agents to automate workflows and drive creative processes
- Data management and governance tactics for operational excellence in media environments
- Attention to privacy and IP through robust cloud security measures
