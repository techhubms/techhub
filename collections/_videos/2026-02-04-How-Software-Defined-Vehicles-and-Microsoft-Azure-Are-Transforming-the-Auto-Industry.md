# How Software-Defined Vehicles and Microsoft Azure Are Transforming the Auto Industry

This exclusive video interview features Ash Sethi, Program Director for Pave 360 at Siemens, sharing insights into the future of automotive innovation. Key topics discussed include:

- **Software-Defined Vehicles**: Exploration of how the automotive industry is moving towards vehicles defined more by their software than hardware, enabling rapid innovation and adaptability.
- **Digital Twins**: Use of digital twin technology for real-time simulation, improving design, testing, and deployment processes for next-generation vehicles.
- **Cloud-Powered Development**: The collaboration between Siemens and Microsoft Azure to build the Pace 360 Automotive platform, which accelerates software development cycles and leverages the scalability and compute power of the cloud.
- **Accelerating Innovation**: Discussion on enabling real-time simulation, faster prototyping, and enhanced safety for autonomous and electric vehicles via the cloud.
