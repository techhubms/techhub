# .NET Conf 2025 - Day 2

.NET Conf celebrates its 15th year as a fully virtual event, offering an engaging and educational platform for .NET developers around the world. With over 100,000 live viewers and hundreds of local events, the conference brings together both community members and the .NET team to share expertise and foster growth within the ecosystem.

## Event Highlights

- **Three Days of Sessions:**
  - Live, expert-led talks and technical sessions
  - Speakers from both the community and the .NET product teams
  - Opportunities to ask questions and interact with presenters in real time

- **Interactive Experiences:**
  - Live Q&A on Twitter and other platforms
  - Virtual attendee parties featuring trivia games and opportunities to win prizes

- **Learning and Resources:**
  - Takeaways to help attendees become better .NET developers
  - Access to official blogs, documentation, forums, and community channels
