# Transforming IT at AT&T with Agentic AI: Microsoft & IBM Partnership

## Overview

This Microsoft Ignite session explores how AT&T, in partnership with Microsoft and IBM, accelerates IT transformation using agentic AI and multi-agent systems. Enterprise-scale AI adoption is shown to drive innovation, resilience, and productivity across complex technology landscapes.

### Key Topics Covered

- **Multi-Agent AI Systems**: Leveraging coordinated AI agents to automate IT delivery and unlock business value.
- **IBM Consulting Advantage Copilot**: An internal agentic tool designed to improve company knowledge access and client insights within IT teams.
- **Scaling AI**: AT&T’s AI deployment serves 100,000 users and processes 10 billion tokens daily, demonstrating advanced scalability.
- **Performance Optimization**: Technical insights into managing cost, accuracy, and latency trade-offs in enterprise AI solutions.
- **Hackathon Initiatives**: Adoption of no-code tools by professional developers, illustrating how democratized AI accelerates innovation.
- **IBM–Microsoft Partnership**: The collaboration enables AT&T’s migration of IT workloads to the cloud, facilitating greater AI integration.
- **AI-Driven Gains**: AT&T and IBM report observed productivity improvements of 30–35% via automation and agentic solutions, alongside significant cost savings.
- **Enterprise Best Practices**: IBM’s role as 'Client Zero' highlights lessons learned from large-scale AI deployments within global enterprises.
