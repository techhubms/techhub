# AI Frontiers with Foundry Labs: Experiment Today, Lead Tomorrow

**Speakers:** Ashley Llorens, Marco Casalaina, Shannon Monroe  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 (Session BRK204)

## Overview

Curious about the future of enterprise AI? Foundry Labs is Microsoft’s platform for rapid experimentation with experimental models, multi-agent orchestration prototypes, and novel AI solutions. This breakout session unveils how researchers and practitioners can leverage Foundry Labs to drive real impact in advanced AI applications.

## Key Highlights & Chapters

- **Trellis Demonstration:** 3D object generation for creative and industrial uses
- **AI Agents & Autogen:** Exploration of agent orchestration concepts and introduction to the Autogen open-source framework with industry adoption examples
- **Global Collaboration:** Insights into Microsoft Research’s partnerships with universities and local ecosystems
- **Showcase Previews:** Sneak peeks at Aurora, MMCT Agent, and projects from external partners
- **Weather Modeling Applications:** Expanding AI-driven modeling from meteorology to agriculture, logistics, and energy, including fine-tuning Aurora for speedy training and temperature prediction
- **Training Corpus:** Utilizing Azure AI Search to support agent-driven research and data access
- **Planner-Critic Architecture:** Introducing a novel approach for multimodal reasoning

## Featured Technologies

- **Microsoft Foundry Labs:** Innovation hub for AI experimentation
- **Azure AI Services:** Central to agent workflows and data exploration
- **Autogen:** Open-source agent orchestration framework
- **Agent Factory & Multi-Agent Systems:** Blueprint for collaborative AI systems
- **Azure AI Search:** Facilitating advanced data discovery

## How Foundry Labs Empowers Researchers

- Fast prototyping and validation of new agent architectures
- Collaboration with leading innovators
- Influence over enterprise-ready AI solutions
