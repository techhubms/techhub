# AI-Driven Insights and Analytics with Microsoft Fabric and Power BI

**Session Speakers:** Lada Hill, Amir Jafari, Maya Shenhav  
**Session Type:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 Breakout (BRK223)

## Overview

This session highlights the power of Microsoft Fabric in connecting organizational data and enabling business teams to benefit from AI-driven insights. Through seamless integration with Power BI and Fabric data agents, organizations can become AI-first by delivering trusted analytics at scale.

## Key Topics

- **Microsoft Fabric**: Unified data platform for the enterprise
- **Power BI Integration**: Delivering AI-powered analytics and easy-to-consume dashboards
- **Data Agents**: Facilitating seamless data connectivity across sources
- **AI-First Organization**: Practical strategies to embed data-driven, machine learning insights
- **Trusted Analytics**: Ensuring reliability and scalability in enterprise environments

## Benefits

- Centralized data for precise decision making
- Accelerated analytics workflow through AI-powered insights
- Automated, scalable delivery of intelligence across the Microsoft ecosystem
- Empowering business users with accessible machine learning and analytics tools
