# Reinventing Manufacturing with Digital Twin: AI-Powered Advances

**Session:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 | PBRK1770
**Speakers:** Gaurica Chacko, Aniket Deshpande, Jonathan Hobgood, Todd Mersch, Prasad Satyavolu

## Overview

This session explores how **digital twins and AI** are driving transformation in manufacturing and supply chain operations. Leaders from across the technology ecosystem share practical insights on accelerating the design, planning, and build-out of modern facilities using data-driven approaches.

## Key Topics & Chapters

- **Introduction and Welcome** (0:00)
- **Industry Trends in Drug Discovery** (10:56)
- **Accelerated Computing, Generative AI, Omniverse Integration** (11:03)
  - Generative AI and Microsoft's cloud services enable advanced analytics and planning
  - NVIDIA Omniverse provides platforms for simulation and employee training
- **Training and Skill Development** (20:20)
  - Simulation applications for real-world training
  - Startup acceleration using the Inception program and SDK models
- **Manufacturing Challenges** (21:55)
  - Workforce attrition and skill gap management
  - Strategies to mitigate operational risks using AI and digital twin insights
- **Scaling Digital Twin Value** (31:15)
  - Challenges in proving ROI and expanding solutions across multiple plants
  - Integration of product and operational data for accelerated launches
- **Reshoring and Empowerment** (41:40)
  - Digital empowerment strategies to scale manufacturing effectively

## AI & Microsoft Technologies

- Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program featured
- Use of **Microsoft Azure** for secure data operations, collaboration, and integration of AI agents
- Real-world examples of generative AI enhancing productivity and efficiency
- Panel highlights practical tactics for maximizing value from Microsoft’s AI ecosystem

## Challenges & Opportunities

- Proving value: Measuring impact of digital twin investments across plants
- Workforce: AI-driven training and upskilling initiatives to address attrition
- Operational scaling: Using Microsoft tools to consolidate data, improve launch cycles, and maintain efficiency
