# Partner: Benefits for Accelerating Software Company Success | PBRK418

**Speakers:** Sandy Gupta, Michael Marzano, Andrew Smith

**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 (Breakout Session | Intermediate)

## Overview

This session provides an in-depth look at the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program (MAICPP), focusing on resources and benefits available to software development companies at all stages—from building new agent solutions to earning certified software designations.

The program supports companies through:

- Skilling opportunities for teams
- Technical resources and guidance, especially for AI application development
- Go-to-market (GTM) benefits
- New investment tiers for certified ISVs
- Azure sponsorship credits for development and proof-of-concept work
- Marketplace rewards and integration with distributor marketplaces via new APIs

## Key Takeaways

- **MAICPP offers skilling, technical resources, and GTM support** specifically tailored for companies creating AI-powered apps and agent solutions.
- **Azure sponsorship credits** are available to support sandbox, demo, and proof-of-concept development.
- **Certified ISVs with strong Marketplace sales qualify for new investment tiers and rewards.**
- **API pilots** will enhance integration between Microsoft Marketplace and distributor platforms, creating broader sales opportunities.
- **Customer propensity scoring and Marketplace rewards** can drive sales and product growth.
