# Driving Business Value with Agentic AI in Microsoft Environments

## Session Overview

This Microsoft Ignite 2025 foundational session demonstrates NTT DATA’s approach to moving organizations out of AI 'pilot mode' into scalable, production-ready solutions using Microsoft technologies. Speakers Charlie Doubek and Geoff Newman share practical strategies for integrating agentic and generative AI with enterprise knowledge bases and automation tools.

## Key Highlights

- **Recent Announcements**: The session opens with recent innovations from the partnership between NTT DATA and Microsoft, highlighting new agentic AI capabilities.
- **Secure AI-Powered Platforms**: Discussion on designing platforms that enable secure AI deployment across industries, ensuring control and data protection.
- **Unified AI for Employee/Customer Experience**: Demonstrates how unified AI solutions can enhance both employee and customer interactions.
- **AI-Powered Communications Portal**: Introduction to a real-world portal powered by AI, designed by NTT DATA for advanced communications.
- **Collaboration Through AI Integration**: Shows how AI agents can facilitate collaboration and provide unified solutions within existing enterprise environments.
- **Customer-Controlled AI Deployments**: Covers methods for building and deploying AI in customer-controlled and secure environments.
- **Strategic Partnership Future**: Insights into the ongoing collaboration between NTT DATA and Microsoft, and their future plans for industrialized AI platforms.

## Practical Takeaways

- Learn pathways for transitioning from AI experimentation to full production solutions.
- Discover frameworks for connecting enterprise teams with AI agents and automation systems.
- See demonstrations of results-driven agentic AI deployments, emphasizing measurable business value and sustainable results.
