# Unlocking AI Practice Scale for Partners with Azure

**Speakers:** Charlie Doubek, Swapnil Khopkar, Kirill Kotlyarenko  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 – Breakout Session PBRK209  
**Level:** Intermediate (200)

## Overview

This session explores how Microsoft partners can accelerate customer impact by leveraging Azure’s complete AI stack. It emphasizes scalable patterns for deploying AI apps and multi-agent solutions, focusing on practical frameworks like AI Centers of Excellence (CoEs), AI Landing Zones, and KPI-driven process transformation.

## Key Discussion Topics

- **AI Scaling Patterns:** Deep dive into proven approaches and learnings drawn from successful partner engagements
- **Azure’s Full-Stack AI Capabilities:** How Azure enables app and agent development, powered by integrated tools and services
- **Partner Success Stories:** Real-world cases of organizations boosting impact through measurable AI adoption
