# Anders Hejlsberg Reacts to The Octoverse 2025 Report

Anders Hejlsberg, the creator of TypeScript and Technical Fellow at Microsoft, provides his insights on the key findings of GitHub's Octoverse 2025 report.

## Key Topics Covered

- **TypeScript and Language Evolution:**
  - Analysis of TypeScript's current state and future trajectory.
  - Discussion on why developer interest may be shifting from TypeScript to Go and what factors are driving the shift.

- **The Changing Landscape of Open Source:**
  - How open source development practices have evolved over the years.
  - Insights into community contributions and the role of large tech companies.

- **AI's Role in Language Creation:**
  - Examination of how artificial intelligence is influencing the way programming languages are designed and adopted.
  - Thoughts on new language features inspired by AI capabilities.

- **AI and Developer Workflow:**
  - Reflections on how AI-driven tools are reshaping day-to-day software development tasks.
  - Discussion of enhanced productivity, automation, and new best practices due to AI adoption.

## Highlights From Octoverse 2025

- Unique statistics and trends from the latest Octoverse report (see [full report](https://gh.io/octoverse)).
- Commentary on the impact of emerging language trends.
- Predictions on how future workflows will be characterized by greater AI integration and collaborative coding.

## About the Speaker

**Anders Hejlsberg** is known for his work on TypeScript, C#, and as a leader in the programming language community at Microsoft.
