# Scaling Innovation in Smart Eyewear and Connected Retail with Azure and AI

This Microsoft Ignite 2025 breakout session (BRKSP491) brings together industry leaders to discuss how cutting-edge Azure services and AI technology are empowering companies like EssilorLuxottica and their new Nuance Audio division to innovate at scale.

## Key Topics

- **AI-Driven Hearing Enhancement**: Advanced beamforming techniques powering Nuance Audio's smart eyewear enhance hearing experiences using real-time AI.
- **Device Management at Scale**: TeamViewer enables secure remote operations—controlling thousands of devices globally via Azure connectivity.
- **Customer Experience Transformation**: Smart glasses deliver a 'wow effect' for end users, backed by satisfaction metrics and real-world feedback.
- **Retail Efficiency & Growth**: Azure-based solutions streamline store technology management, driving sales and operational efficiency.

## Technology Partnership

- Collaboration between TeamViewer, Microsoft Azure, and EssilorLuxottica highlights technology alignment, buy/build/partner decision-making, and the strategic value of cloud platforms for global retail markets.
