# Exploring the New Microsoft Foundry Portal: A Guided Tour

**Author:** April (Microsoft Developer)

## Overview

In this video, April demonstrates the upgraded Microsoft Foundry portal, focusing on its new user experience and essential features for developers interested in building with Azure AI. The walkthrough highlights how to access the portal, initiate new projects, and leverage integrated tools for agent development, operation, and AI-powered search.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Accessing the New Portal:** Instructions for logging into https://ai.azure.com and an orientation to the welcome screen.
- **Project Creation:** Step-by-step on starting new projects and setting up your workspace.
- **Navigation Tour:**
  - **Discover:** Explore what’s possible in Foundry, find resources, and get inspiration.
  - **Build:** Use integrated tools to develop and test AI agents or models.
  - **Operate:** Manage your projects, deployments, and monitor their health.
  - **Ask AI & Search:** Harness Foundry’s AI features for troubleshooting or learning more about the platform.
