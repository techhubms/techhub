# AI Enterprise Value: Real-World Applications

## Session Overview

Microsoft Ignite 2025's breakout session (BRK334) focuses on how enterprises are driving business value through practical AI applications powered by Microsoft Foundry and the Model Context Protocol (MCP) on Windows. Experts from Microsoft and leading consultancies discuss successful strategies for integrating AI solutions, sharing implementation guidance and real-world examples.

### Key Takeaways

- **Global AI Adoption Statistics & Economic Projections**: The session opens with the latest data on enterprise AI adoption and its projected impact on business economics.
- **Real-World Integration Strategies**: App developers, consulting firms, and enterprise teams share experiences building intelligent solutions that accelerate workflows and digitally transform operations.
- **Practical Guidance for Developers and Integrators**:
  - Opportunities for technical teams to leverage AI endpoints via Microsoft Foundry and MCP.
  - Actionable steps to start small and prototype AI projects for rapid value delivery.
  - End-user experience as a key driver over technical implementation.
- **Case Study Highlights**:
  - **Speech-to-Form Technology for Traders**: Details on deploying natural language technology to streamline complex workflows in trading environments.
  - **Agentic AI and Local AI**: Insights from partner discussions about deploying agentic architectures and edge AI solutions.
