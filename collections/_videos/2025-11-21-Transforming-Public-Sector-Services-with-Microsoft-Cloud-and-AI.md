# Transforming Public Sector Services with Microsoft Cloud and AI

Governments face mounting pressures to modernize, build trust, improve citizen engagement, and deliver efficient services. In this Microsoft Ignite 2025 session, speakers from Microsoft and partners showcase practical applications of cloud and AI technologies designed for public sector needs.

## Key Government Challenges

- Workforce management
- Trust and security requirements
- Infrastructure modernization

## Technology-Driven Priorities

- **San Francisco:** Deploying technology to support safety, economic vitality, and affordability
- **Raleigh:** Using urban mobility data to manage growth and improve citizen outcomes

## AI Solutions for Service Delivery

- Bringing AI to frontline workers to enhance service provision
- Using AI to streamline government processes and improve customer experience

## Case Studies

- **City of Burlington:** AI-powered citizen services for improved engagement and efficiency
- **Esri Collaboration:** Leveraging GIS analytics and Microsoft platforms for data-driven urban management
- **Tomorrow.io:** Innovative approaches to government challenges with weather and infrastructure analytics

## Framework for Transformation

- Four pillars: secure infrastructure, scalable platforms, compliance, and future-ready AI transformation
