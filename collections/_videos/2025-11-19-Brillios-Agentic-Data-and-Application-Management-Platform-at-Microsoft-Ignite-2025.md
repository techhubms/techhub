# Brillio's Agentic Data and Application Management Platform | Microsoft Ignite 2025

**Session ID:** ODSP1454
**Speaker:** Muthumari S
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025

## Overview

Brillio’s ADAM (Agentic Data and Application Management) platform is designed to address the challenges enterprises face in AI strategy, platform management, and talent. As a composable and tech-agnostic solution, ADAM accelerates the shift from human-in-the-loop AI models to self-learning, agentic systems.

### Main Topics Covered

- **Transitioning from Human-in-the-Loop to Self-Learning Systems**
  - Examines evolution from manual oversight AI workflows to self-improving agent-based systems.

- **Introducing ADAM**
  - Describes ADAM’s architecture and its core engine powering enterprise AI services.

- **Understanding AI Pillars Through Conversational Interface**
  - Demonstrates how ADAM leverages conversational interfaces to simplify strategy and decision-making.

- **Strategy Toolkit for AI Roadmapping**
  - Showcases ADAM’s built-in tools for developing and executing AI strategies within organizations.

- **Foundational Solutions and Prebuilt Agents**
  - Offers insight into turnkey solutions and reusable agent components for rapid deployment.

- **Domain Intelligent Business Solutions**
  - Highlights specialized business solutions built on ADAM's platform for diverse industries.

- **ADAM Control Tower**
  - Presents the centralized command center for managing, monitoring, and optimizing AI operations.

## Session Notes

This session provides practical guidance and a strategic overview for IT professionals and enterprise AI leaders seeking to adopt agentic architectures and composable AI platforms. Key takeaways include:

- Benefits of moving towards self-learning, agentic systems for scalability and efficiency.
- How strategy toolkits and control towers support governance and AI lifecycle management.
- Where conversational AI interfaces can simplify operations and improve user adoption.

## About the Speaker

**Muthumari S** shares real-world insights from Brillio’s enterprise deployments, emphasizing actionable strategies for leveraging ADAM in solving the AI trilemma and advancing agentic approaches in business solutions.
