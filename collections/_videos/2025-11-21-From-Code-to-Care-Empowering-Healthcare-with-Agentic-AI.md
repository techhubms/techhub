# From Code to Care: Empowering Healthcare with Agentic AI

**Speakers:** Andrew Soltan, Naveen Valluri  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 – BRK373 (Foundational/100)  

## Session Overview

This session explores how Microsoft Azure's AI capabilities—including both low-code and pro-code tools—enable the rapid creation of specialized AI agents to address real-world healthcare challenges. By harnessing unified healthcare data and integrated security, development teams are streamlining workflows and surfacing actionable insights for clinicians.

## Key Topics Covered

### Oxford Collaboration and Cancer Care

- Deep dive into Oxford's regional cancer services and large-scale hospital operations
- 2023 challenge: leveraging agentic AI to improve cancer treatment planning efficiency
- Real use cases: multidisciplinary team (MDT) workflow automation using AI agents
- Ongoing and future opportunities for AI in oncology and broader healthcare

### Architecture and Solutions

- Unified data and Azure AI underpin scalable, secure deployments
- Presentation of agentic AI architectural patterns for healthcare applications
- Addressing challenges in integrating AI across multiple clinical applications and legacy systems (Medicare mentioned)

### Ethical Considerations

- Building trust in AI-powered healthcare decisions
- Ensuring responsible, explainable, and ethical use of AI technology

### Scalability and Reusability

- Discussion on how to design agentic AI solutions that are scalable beyond oncology and adaptable to other services
- Focus on architectural best practices for modularity and reusability
