# Blazor Community Standup: ASP.NET Core & Blazor Roadmap for .NET 11

Presented by Daniel Roth and Javier Calvarro Nelson, this video session walks through the planned improvements and upcoming features for ASP.NET Core and Blazor as part of the .NET 11 release. Key topics covered include:

- **Expected Improvements in .NET 11**: A summary of features and enhancements targeting performance, developer productivity, and modern web development paradigms.
- **Current Progress Update**: An update on ongoing development work, with discussion of what's already delivered and what remains in progress.
- **Developer Impact**: Insights into how these improvements will affect the developer experience and recommended practices for adopting new features.
- **Community Engagement**: Opportunity for feedback, questions, and community discussion regarding the direction of Blazor and ASP.NET Core.

[Links and Additional Resources](https://www.theurlist.com/aspnet-standup-20260210)

This session is ideal for developers building with Microsoft technologies who are interested in staying current with the latest advancements in .NET, ASP.NET Core, and Blazor.
