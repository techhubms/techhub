# Real Stories of AI Transformation in Local Government

## Overview

This intermediate-level Microsoft Ignite 2025 session (BRK369) features direct insights from city and government leaders about their technical journeys with Microsoft technologies.

### Session Highlights

- **Key Issues:** Legacy systems, workforce optimization, cybersecurity.
- **Speakers:** Brett Howard, Michael Makstman, Angel Smith, with spotlight interviews including Mike Makoski, CIO of San Francisco, and Andrew Hester from Raleigh.
- **AI Innovation:** Positioning San Francisco as a leader in responsible and equitable AI adoption supported by Microsoft platforms.
- **Use Cases:** Projects range from citizen engagement to AI-based energy permitting accelerators and city infrastructure scaling.
- **Technical Strategies:** Insights into workforce readiness, data modernization, security measures, and responsible AI practices.
- **Security and Data Sovereignty:** Discussion about protecting sensitive city data and ensuring compliance.

## Chapters Summary

- **Legacy Systems and Cybersecurity**: Outlining the foundational challenges faced by city IT teams.
- **San Francisco IT Operations**: Introduction to municipal technology leadership and details about authentic AI adoption.
- **Equitable and Responsible AI**: Frameworks for fostering fairness and technical readiness in government workforce.
- **Citizen Engagement**: Using Microsoft tools for improving access and services to local populations.
- **AI-Based Accelerator**: Case study on optimizing energy permitting through AI integration.
- **Scaling City Infrastructure**: Interview with Raleigh's Andrew Hester about project outcomes and strategic vision for the future.
- **Focus Areas**: Deep dives into security, data sovereignty, and robust AI governance using Microsoft solutions.

## Lessons Learned

- Effective modernization requires clear strategy, stakeholder buy-in, and robust data governance.
- Equitable access and responsible AI are priorities for city innovation.
- Security and sovereignty must be baked into digital transformation utilizing Microsoft Azure and AI technologies.
