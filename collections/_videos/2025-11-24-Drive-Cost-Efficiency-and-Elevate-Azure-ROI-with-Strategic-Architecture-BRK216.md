# From Spend to Strategy: Drive Cost Efficiency and Elevate Azure ROI (BRK216)

**Speakers:** Rob Kuehfus, Mrunal Patwa

**Session Overview:**
This Microsoft Ignite breakout session targets architects and technical strategists aiming to maximize the business value of their Azure investments. Session highlights include:

- Strategic approaches for building long-term business value with Azure
- Eight foundational design areas covering environment and compliance considerations
- Landing zone hierarchy and policy inheritance for robust cost control
- Demonstrations of Copilot prompts for modernization and cost-aware architecture goals
- Deployment walkthrough to Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS)
- Utilizing the Azure Hybrid Benefit to capture additional savings
- Cloud-native application architecture templates for accurate cost estimation
- Live Copilot-assisted recommendations for automated optimization and cost reduction

## Key Topics

### 1. Building for Sustainable Business Value

- Aligning cloud investments with organizational goals
- Ensuring architecture foundation supports long-term ROI

### 2. Design Areas & Compliance

- Advanced (300 level) coverage of environment setup
- Policy management and compliance strategies to support efficiency

### 3. Cost Control Strategies

- Establishing landing zones in Azure for effective resource management
- Policy inheritance as a mechanism for maintaining budget discipline

### 4. Modernization with Copilot

- Prompt engineering for cost-aware modernization goals
- Copilot's automated planning and implementation support
- Deploying solutions to AKS with Copilot assistance

### 5. Azure Hybrid Benefit Utilization

- Reducing costs through licensing offers and hybrid capabilities

### 6. Automated Optimization & Recommendations

- Using cloud-native templates for consistent architecture and cost estimates
- Copilot-driven insights to identify and implement cost-saving actions
