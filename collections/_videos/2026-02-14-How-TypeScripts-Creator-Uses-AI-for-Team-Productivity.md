**Featured Speaker:** Anders Hejlsberg (TypeScript creator)

In this video, Anders Hejlsberg describes the practical benefits the TypeScript team enjoys by integrating AI into their development processes. A key example highlighted is the use of AI to automate the tedious and repetitive work of porting hundreds of pull requests from an old codebase to a new one.
