# Agentic AI Drives Excellence in Financial Services: Microsoft Ignite Panel Highlights

## Overview

This Microsoft Ignite 2025 breakout session features a panel of experts from global financial institutions discussing the ways agentic and generative AI are reshaping the finance industry. The session emphasizes practical lessons from large-scale deployments and outlines strategic initiatives for organizations becoming AI-first.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Agentic AI in Finance**: Industry leaders discuss leveraging AI to improve customer experiences, drive operational resilience, and boost risk intelligence.
- **Generative AI in Banking Processes**: Real-world examples showcase how AI supports end-to-end banking operations.
- **AI-First Organizations**: OP Bank details its vision and experiences as an early adopter of AI at scale.
- **Modernizing Infrastructure**: The panel covers the shift from traditional data centers to cloud-based solutions, highlighting the role of application consolidation.
- **Technical Debt & Code Modernization**: Learn how agentic code development is used to address and reduce technical debt within financial organizations.
- **Unified Experience Strategy**: The importance of delivering cohesive experiences for both members and employees in a digital-first setting.
- **Microsoft Tools for AI Transformation**: The panel identifies Microsoft products, including Copilot, as crucial to change management and successful AI rollout.
