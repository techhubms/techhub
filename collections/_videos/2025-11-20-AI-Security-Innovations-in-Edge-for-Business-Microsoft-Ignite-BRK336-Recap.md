# AI Security Innovations in Edge for Business: Microsoft Ignite BRK336 Recap

## Session Overview

This session, presented at Microsoft Ignite 2025 by Lindsay Kubasik and Sean Lyndersay, introduces Edge for Business as a secure, AI-driven enterprise browser. The session details how Edge for Business integrates advanced AI features like Copilot Mode, multi-tab reasoning, and agentic browsing to streamline workflows and enhance productivity.

## Key Highlights

- **Copilot Mode & Agentic AI Browsing:**
  - Copilot Mode adds proactive, context-aware AI assistance directly into the browser.
  - Agentic browsing allows users to operate in an 'agent mode,' delegating complex research or workflow tasks to AI assistants.
- **Demo:**
  - The session includes a day-in-the-life demonstration of how these AI features help users like Adele summarize data and send results via Microsoft Teams.
- **Enterprise Security Focus:**
  - Edge for Business is positioned as a leading secure enterprise browser, designed with security at its core.
  - Existing data protection features are showcased, including integration with Microsoft Purview Data Loss Prevention (DLP).
- **Recognition:**
  - Microsoft is highlighted as a leader in the enterprise browser market, with commitments to both productivity and security.

## Architectural Insights

- Edge for Business combines AI-powered productivity solutions with compliance-grade security, catering to organizations that demand both innovation and protection.
- Data protection strategies and features like Purview DLP ensure enterprise requirements for governance and compliance are met.
