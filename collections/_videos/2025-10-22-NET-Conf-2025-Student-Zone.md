# .NET Conf 2025 - Student Zone

.NET Conf celebrates its 15th annual online conference, continuing as a global virtual event for .NET developers. Over three days, attendees can join a variety of live sessions hosted by community members and the official .NET team.

## Event Highlights

- **Live Sessions:** Diverse presentations on .NET topics suitable for all skill levels.
- **Community Engagement:** Opportunity to interact directly with speakers and fellow attendees on platforms like Twitter and Twitch.
- **Virtual Parties:** Participate in trivia games and win prizes at virtual attendee gatherings.
- **Learning Opportunities:** Sessions designed to help you become a better .NET developer, with content suited for students and experienced professionals.
