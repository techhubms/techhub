# How can AI help me modernize my app?

Modernizing apps sounds great—until you have to do the work.

Matt Soucoup explains how **GitHub Copilot** can help you move a legacy **.NET** application toward modern .NET by:

- **Analyzing your project** to understand what you have today
- **Generating a modernization plan** to outline what to change and in what order
- **Creating task lists** so the work becomes actionable and trackable

The pitch is that Copilot can help turn modernization from a “someday project” into something you can actually ship.
