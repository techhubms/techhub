# Welcome to .NET 10 & Visual Studio 2026

## Keynote Highlights

Presented by Scott Hanselman and members of the .NET and Visual Studio teams, this keynote marks the launch of:

- **.NET 10**: The most intelligent, unified release yet
- **Visual Studio 2026**: Faster, smarter development environment
- **C# 14** and **F# 10**: Enhanced language expressiveness and functionality
- **.NET Aspire 13**: Simplified orchestration for cloud-native apps
- **ASP.NET Core 10**: Secure, modern, full-stack web development
- **.NET MAUI 10**: Cross-platform app development improvements

## What You'll See

- Productivity and language enhancements in C# 14 and F# 10
- New app development capabilities for cloud-native, web, and cross-platform targets
- Modern orchestration tools for simpler deployments with .NET Aspire
- Security and full-stack development using ASP.NET Core 10
- Unified developer experience with Visual Studio 2026
