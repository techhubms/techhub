# Microsoft IQ Overview: Work IQ, Foundry IQ, and Fabric IQ

**Author:** John Savill's Technical Training

## Introduction

This presentation offers a comprehensive overview of Microsoft IQ, a suite of AI-powered capabilities designed to enhance enterprise knowledge management. The discussion covers the foundational concepts of enterprise information, the necessity of contextual knowledge, and dives deep into the functionalities of Work IQ, Foundry IQ, and Fabric IQ.

### Key Topics Covered

- **AI Model Knowledge:** Understanding what constitutes the foundation for enterprise intelligence.
- **Types of Enterprise Information:** Classification and relevance for business needs.
- **The Need for Enterprise Context:** Why contextual knowledge is critical for AI-driven solutions.
- **Microsoft IQ Overview:** Introduction to the concept and its various components.
- **Work IQ, Foundry IQ, Fabric IQ:** Differentiating between the services and their specific enterprise applications.
- **Using Microsoft IQ:** How organizations can utilize Microsoft IQ to advance their knowledge management strategies.
- **Agents and Agent 365:** Overview of agents utilizing Microsoft IQ capabilities to automate and support enterprise operations.

## Microsoft IQ and Enterprise AI

Microsoft IQ provides a framework for managing and retrieving enterprise data using AI models specialized for context and organizational knowledge. Work IQ, Foundry IQ, and Fabric IQ represent tailored modules targeting specific enterprise challenges, such as workflow automation, foundational AI model integration, and data fabric orchestration, respectively.

Agents, including Agent 365, leverage these modules to deliver automated assistance, advanced search, and contextual responses within enterprise environments.
