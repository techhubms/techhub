# Empowering Partners to Navigate the New Security Landscape

**Session: PBRK273 | Ignite 2025**  
Speakers: Naohiro Fukatani, Heather Gordon, Herain Oberoi (VP of Data and AI Security at Microsoft)

## Overview

This foundational session at Microsoft Ignite 2025 equips partners—including those working with Copilot, Azure, or new to security—with actionable knowledge to integrate and sell Microsoft’s end-to-end security portfolio. The talk addresses the evolving security landscape, customer conversations about AI and data security, and effective go-to-market strategies.

## Key Security Challenges Discussed

- **Agent Sprawl**: Managing the complexity of multiple security agents and toolsets
- **Data Leaks**: Mitigating risks related to both internal and external data exposures
- **Shadow AI Threats**: Recognizing and addressing unsanctioned AI solutions and potential vulnerabilities
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Adapting to evolving legal and compliance requirements for enterprise data

## Microsoft Security Product Suite

- **Microsoft Sentinel**: SIEM and SOAR platform for intelligent threat detection and response
- **Microsoft Purview**: Tools for data governance, protection, and compliance management
- **Microsoft Entra ID (Azure Active Directory)**: Identity and access management for secure applications
- **Microsoft Intune**: Endpoint security and device management
- **Microsoft Defender Suite**: Comprehensive solutions for threat protection and security management across devices and services

## Collaboration and Shared Responsibilities

Partners are encouraged to facilitate collaboration across IT, security, and development teams. Discussion emphasized shared security models and best practices within organizations.

## Partner Success and Next Steps

- **Strategic Evolution**: Insights from JBS on leveraging Microsoft’s move from EMS to E5 licensing and incorporating Entra ID for advanced identity solutions
- **Skills and Designations**: Guidance on developing specialized skills and attaining designations to build trust and drive co-selling opportunities
- **Partner Tools and Recognition**: Access partner tools via the Microsoft AI Partner Centre; leverage official badges for partner recognition

## Competitive Edge

- Discussion of Microsoft’s strengths in enterprise security, emphasizing their robust threat intelligence and ecosystem
