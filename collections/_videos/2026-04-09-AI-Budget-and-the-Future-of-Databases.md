# AI, Budget, and the Future of Databases

In this Budget Bites finale, Microsoft leaders discuss what building AI-powered applications on a budget signals for developers today—starting from a **$25 AI app challenge** and extending to **production scaling** using **Azure SQL**, **GitHub Copilot**, and **intelligent agents**.

## Guests

- **Shireesh** (CVP, Azure Databases)
- **Priya Sati** (VP, SQL)

- **25:02** – Final takeaways and building budget-smart AI apps
