# .NET Data Community Standup: EF 10 Release Celebration

Join the Entity Framework (EF) engineering team for a special community standup to celebrate the release of EF 10. This session features Jiri Cincura and other members of the EF team, offering an inside look at what's new in EF 10, including:

- An overview of major features and improvements in EF 10
- Q&A with the EF engineering team
- Behind-the-scenes discussion on the release process
- Tips and best practices for .NET developers using EF Core

**Topics Covered:**

- New data access capabilities in EF 10
- Enhancements to performance and developer experience
- Compatibility and migration from previous EF Core versions
- Real-world usage scenarios, and lessons from the team

Whether you're just starting with Entity Framework or are a seasoned .NET developer, this standup delivers actionable knowledge to elevate your applications using EF 10.

**Featuring:** Jiri Cincura and the Entity Framework team

For more resources and to join future community sessions, visit the official EF and .NET channels.
