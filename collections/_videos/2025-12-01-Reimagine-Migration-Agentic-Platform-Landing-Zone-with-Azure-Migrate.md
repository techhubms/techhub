# Reimagine Migration: Agentic Platform Landing Zone with Azure Migrate

Azure cloud migration projects are often hindered by manual errors and complexities, leaving organizations vulnerable to inconsistent environments and compliance challenges. In this episode of the Azure Essentials Show, Thomas Maurer and Shiva Shastri discuss how Azure Migrate’s automated, agentic approach transforms the migration process.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Typical Migration Challenges**: Manual steps introduce inconsistency and increase compliance risk during workload migration.
- **Platform Landing Zones Defined**: Introduction to Azure Landing Zones as secure, compliant environments based on Microsoft’s best practices.
- **Agentic Approach with Azure Migrate**: Demonstration of how Azure Migrate guides and automates migration to Landing Zones, minimizing manual errors.
- **Guided Migration Experience**: Overview of the step-by-step, automated deployment and its benefits for modernizing on Azure.
- **Demo Walkthrough**: Practical demo showing how workloads can be transitioned smoothly and securely.
- **Resources**: Access to further reading, preview sign-ups, and related episodes.
