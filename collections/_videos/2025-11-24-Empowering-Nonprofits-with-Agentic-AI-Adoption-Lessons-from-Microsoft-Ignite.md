# Empowering Nonprofits with Agentic AI: Adoption Lessons from Microsoft Ignite

**Session Overview**

Microsoft Ignite 2025 featured a breakout session on nonprofit adoption of agentic AI technologies. The speakers, including Morteza Ghazai, provide insights into how agentic AI is currently impacting program management, fundraising, and operations within various nonprofit organizations.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Real-World Examples:** Mathis, Opportunity International, and Worldfish showcase successful implementations of agentic AI.
- **Multi-Agent Systems:** Highlights the emergence of multi-agent AI for improved task coordination and operational efficiency.
- **Custom Solutions:** Examples such as Big Brothers Big Sisters of Puget Sound illustrate agent-driven proposal customization, risk assessment, and trend detection.
- **Grant Evaluation Dashboard:** Demonstrates a dashboard powered by multi-agent AI for grant evaluation.
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Emphasizes reference architecture availability and the importance of sharing lessons among nonprofit peers.
- **Overcoming Resistance:** Key strategies for overcoming resistance to digital transformation and fostering AI leadership within organizations.

## Practical Strategies

- Integrating AI agents in nonprofit operations for mission-critical outcomes.
- Assessing organizational readiness and addressing barriers such as resistance to change.
- Using reference architectures to accelerate adoption and learning from early adopters.
- Encouragement toward community-wide knowledge sharing and digital leadership.

## Q&A Highlights

The session concludes with a Q&A, focusing on best practices and real-time adoption feedback from nonprofit institutions.
