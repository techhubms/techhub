# .NET Data Community Standup: Introduction to Microsoft.Extensions.DataIngestion

In this session, Adam Sitnik, who worked directly on the implementation, introduces the new Microsoft.Extensions.DataIngestion library. Hosted by Jiri Cincura and Shay Rojansky, the discussion covers:

- **Purpose and use-cases**: Data ingestion workflows, working with documents, and supporting scenarios involving parsing and transformation.
- **Integration with .NET**: How the new extension fits into the broader Microsoft.Extensions ecosystem for .NET developers.
- **AI Features**: Discussion of processing vectors and document embeddings, as well as the relation to AI-driven data solutions.
- **Practical insights**: Architecture decisions, design patterns, and behind-the-scenes development notes provided by Adam Sitnik.
- **Community engagement**: The .NET Data Community’s role in shaping the library’s direction and use-cases.

## Key Topics

- Data ingestion pipelines for .NET
- Parsing and transforming documents
- Handling vectors and embeddings in modern data applications
- Practical demo scenarios and real-world applications

## About the Speakers

- **Adam Sitnik**: Core developer of Microsoft.Extensions.DataIngestion
- **Jiri Cincura** and **Shay Rojansky**: Community leaders and hosts
