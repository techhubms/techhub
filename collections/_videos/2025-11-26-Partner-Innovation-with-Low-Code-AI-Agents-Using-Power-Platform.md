# Partner Innovation with Low-Code AI Agents Using Power Platform

**Session Overview**

This breakout session from Microsoft Ignite 2025 discusses how partners can leverage Power Platform and AI to lead digital transformation:

- **Low-Code App Development:** Discover how Power Apps enables rapid creation of business applications with minimal coding.
- **Process Automation:** Learn to automate workflows and repetitive tasks using Power Automate and connected AI agents.
- **AI Agents:** Explore integration of AI agents that extend the capabilities of apps and automation solutions, opening new possibilities for scaling business practices.

**Key Topics:**

- Unlocking new business value through partner innovation
- Scaling solutions and practices to meet evolving market needs
- Delivering customer impact with agile technology adoption
- Becoming a catalyst for change in the AI era
