# From VMs and Containers to AI Apps with Azure Red Hat OpenShift

**Speakers:** Phelipi Dal’Olio Santos, Mayuri Gupta, Campbell Vertesi
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 (BRK104)
**Level:** Intermediate (200)

## Overview

Learn how organizations can:

- Simplify IT operations
- Lower operational costs
- Run containers and virtual machines on a unified, flexible platform using Red Hat OpenShift Virtualization on Azure Red Hat OpenShift (ARO)
- Embrace and evolve their AI strategy

## Key Topics Covered

- **Business Challenges:** Address cost management, security concerns, and adoption of AI
- **Azure Flexibility & Innovation:** Insights into how Azure helps meet varied customer needs by providing adaptable cloud solutions
- **Migration Considerations:** Covers efficiency improvement, hybrid benefit adoption, and MAC (migration acceleration commitment) deduction
- **Demo:** Includes a demonstration of cluster administration tools, use of deployed operators for virtualization and migration, lifecycle management of VMs through YAML and configuration controls, and setting up migration providers and plans

## AI in Action: Bradesco Case Study

- Real-world example of how Banco Bradesco is using Microsoft Foundry on ARO
- Focus on enterprise-grade AI innovation, security, and scalability
- Highlights the use of Arrow and AI Bridge for integrating multiple AI models across Azure and with external partners
- Key lessons learned: the importance of strong foundation and governance from the outset
