# Empowering Healthcare Through Scalable AI for Trusted Partners

**Presented by:** Cornelis Hertogh, Feliz Fuentes Montpellier  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025  
**Session:** PBRK390  

## Overview

This session explores opportunities for Microsoft partners to harness the latest AI, data, and platform innovations for healthcare. Emphasis is placed on how Microsoft's unified marketplace, investments in responsible AI, and partner programs empower partners to build scalable and intelligent healthcare solutions.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Types of AI in Healthcare:**
  - *Ambient AI*: Context-aware solutions that support care environments
  - *Generative AI*: Automated content creation for clinical and workflow enhancement
  - *Agentic AI*: Intelligent agents for specialized healthcare tasks

- **Microsoft’s AI Investments:**
  - Responsible AI frameworks and guidance
  - Specialized solutions (including Dragon Copilot global expansion and roadmap)
  - Partner programs that provide training and app specialization incentives
  - Workflow migration support for healthcare partners

- **Partner Ecosystem & Marketplace:**
  - Unified platform to distribute and extend AI solutions
  - ISV resources and partner training portals

- **Healthcare Data and Applications:**
  - Addressing diverse data sources, including imaging
  - Building predictive and specialized AI solutions for clinical use
  - Incentivizing clinical app specialization through the CSP program
