# Premier League’s Data-Driven Fan Engagement at Scale

**Speakers:** Darren Hardman, Simon Brown, Pedro Gomes, Akash Jain  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 (Session BRK227)

## Overview

The Premier League, in partnership with Microsoft, has transformed decades of match data, editorial content, and video into a real-time, intelligent, and personalized experience for fans worldwide. This session reveals how scalable architecture, data strategy, and AI orchestration can modernize engagement for the world's most-watched football league.

## Key Topics

- **Data Strategy:** Approaches for unifying historic and live match data, editorial stories, and video archives.
- **AI Orchestration:** Leveraging Microsoft’s AI capabilities to create real-time insights and recommendations.
- **Scalable Architecture:** Building solutions that serve millions of global fans with high availability and performance.
- **Personalization:** Delivering tailored experiences and content using data-driven intelligence.

## Solutions & Technologies

- **Microsoft Fabric:** As the backbone of the data platform, Fabric enables seamless integration, governance, and scalability.
- **Azure Cloud Services:** For computing, data processing, and AI workloads.
- **Real-Time Processing:** Enabling instant data analysis and personalized recommendations.

## Actionable Lessons

- Blueprint strategies for large-scale data integration and AI adoption.
- Steps to unify disparate data sources and deliver actionable, personalized insights.
- Architectures for building high-performance, scalable data platforms for consumer engagement.
