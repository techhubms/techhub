# Advancing Industrial Agility with Agentic AI

**Session: BRK374 | Microsoft Ignite 2025**

## Overview

Agentic AI is rapidly evolving from a buzzword to an essential tool for industrial acceleration. This session explores how AI agents and multi-agent systems enhance operations, boost decision-making, and generate new revenue streams across sectors like Energy, Manufacturing, Consumer Goods, and Mobility.

### Key Topics Discussed

- **Data as Centerpiece**: The blend of physical and digital transformation is driven by data at the core of industrial advances.
- **Defining Industrial Autonomy**: Presentation by ABB’s Rajesh Ramachandran outlines industrial autonomy's twin pillars: operational flexibility and safety.
- **Evolving Agentic Automation**: Exploration of multi-agent systems as part of future automation strategies.
- **Industrial AI Evolution & Impact**: Recap of progress and measurable impacts on customer operations from implementing industrial AI solutions.
- **Machine Operators & Digital Twins**: Empowering skilled operators and technicians through AI-driven digital twins.
- **AI-driven Revenue Streams**: How organizations use AI to innovate service delivery and generate entirely new business models.
- **Implementation Approaches**: Analysis of implementation differences between manufacturing processes and customer-facing AI solutions.
