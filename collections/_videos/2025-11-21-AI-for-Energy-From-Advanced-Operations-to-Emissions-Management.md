# AI for Energy: From Advanced Operations to Emissions Management

**Speakers:** Todd Inlander, Hany Soliman  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025  
**Level:** Intermediate (200)

This session explores how Microsoft is empowering energy companies to address decarbonization and operational challenges using AI-powered solutions.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Methane Emissions Detection:** Applying AI to enhance monitoring and reduce environmental impact through early detection.
- **Predictive Maintenance:** Using AI algorithms to forecast equipment health, reducing downtime and improving safety.
- **Generative AI for Permitting:** Streamlining complex compliance processes with generative AI agents for documentation and workflow automation.
- **Agentic AI and Copilots:** Demonstrating the impact of AI agents and copilots for improving compliance, operational efficiency, and emissions management.
- **Industry Case Study – SCE’s AI Journey:** Includes wildfire mitigation, computer vision adoption, and how Copilot enhances energy operations.
- **Future Vision:** Exploring digital twins for utilities, autonomous grid control, and AI-driven operational models.
- **Microsoft Partnerships:** Collaborative projects on transformer monitoring and unifying energy data with AI-driven insights.
