# Understand How Purview Secures and Governs Your Entire Data Estate

## Overview

In this episode of the Azure Essentials Show, Microsoft Azure's Thomas and Darren examine the growing importance of data security and governance in today’s organizations as data volumes explode across multiple platforms and cloud environments.

## Why Traditional Security Isn’t Enough

- Traditional infrastructure security cannot fully address the unique challenges of modern data estates.
- The focus must shift to securing data itself, rather than just infrastructure.

## Introducing Microsoft Purview

- Microsoft Purview is presented as a unified solution for data security, governance, and compliance.
- It enables organizations to manage, secure, and govern their data estate across on-premises, multi-cloud, and SaaS environments.

## The Unified Catalog Feature

- The Unified Catalog streamlines data discovery and access for both technical and business users.
- Simplifies compliance by providing a holistic view of data resources and governance policies.
- The video features a demo showcasing how the catalog makes it easy to locate, classify, and secure sensitive data.

## Best Practices in Data Governance

- Discusses actionable strategies for implementing robust governance policies.
- Emphasizes continuous assessment, role-based access, and compliance monitoring.
