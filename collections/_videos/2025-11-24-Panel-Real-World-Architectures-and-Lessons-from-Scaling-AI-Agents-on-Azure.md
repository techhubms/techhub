# Panel: Real-World Architectures and Lessons from Scaling AI Agents on Azure

**Speakers:** Mark Austin, Luis Héctor Chávez, Adam Orentlicher, Naga Surendran  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 – Session BRK114

## Overview

This session provides an in-depth look at how enterprise teams are building and deploying AI agent solutions using open frameworks and Azure services. The panel explores:

- Architectural fundamentals for scalable agent solutions
- Model selection and evaluation strategies (including Microsoft Foundry)
- Production readiness: orchestration, reliability, trust, and security
- Human-in-the-loop systems and governance for responsible AI
- Autoscaling, fault tolerance, and observability in real deployments
- Best practices and key advice for AI adoption and iterative prototyping

---

## Key Session Topics

### Scaling AI Agents Responsibly

- Strategies for architecting scalable, reliable agentic systems
- Balancing platform reuse with customization for business impact
- Ensuring governance and trust throughout the lifecycle

### Human-in-the-Loop AI Design

- Integrating expert oversight in training and production
- Designing systems for continuous feedback and improvement

### From Prototypes to Production

- Emphasis on live demos and interactive models over static presentations
- Iterative prototyping to accelerate learning and mitigate risk

### Model Choices and Multi-Model Approaches

- Using Microsoft Foundry to evaluate and select the right models for each use case
- Multi-model strategies for matching requirements to capabilities

### Architecture for Resilience and Observability

- Building systems with autoscaling and fault tolerance
- Handling GPU scaling and planning for model failover
- Observability practices for trustworthy operations

---

## Practical Lessons & Takeaways

- Security and orchestration are ongoing production concerns
- Measurable business outcomes require an iterative and outcome-driven approach
- Teams should focus on platform capabilities, not reinventing the wheel
