# Unleashing Telco Innovation: Agentic AI & Connected Intelligence

**Speakers:** Rick Lievano, Gustavo do Couto Duarte, Mathew George  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025, Session BRK385

## Overview

This foundational breakout session demonstrates how the telecommunications industry is reshaping operations by adopting Microsoft AI and cloud technologies. Experts discuss the practical steps telcos take to unify data, automate workflows, accelerate service delivery, and develop secure, autonomous networking capabilities.

## Key Topics

- **TM Forum Standards & Network Automation Levels**: Introduction to industry standards guiding automation.
- **Agent Integration & Model Tuning**: Learnings from the integration of AI agents, insights from model tuning, and leveraging ML analytics.
- **Partnered AI & Automation Benefits**: Panel discussion on how partner solutions boost network efficiency and business agility.
- **Nokia & Industry Automation**: Real-world perspectives on automation benefits and strategic industry shifts.
- **AI Agent Development**: Current and future focus areas for developing agentic AI within telecom workflows, including employee-driven innovation.
- **Security Integration**: How security agents are deployed to safeguard autonomous network systems and data.
- **Recommendations for Telcos**: Actionable advice for organizations starting with autonomous networking and AI-driven operations.
