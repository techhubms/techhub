# .NET MAUI Community Standup: Live from MAUI Day London

Join the MAUI Day crew and supporters for a live community standup streamed from Microsoft Paddington, London. The session features David Ortinau and Gerald Versluis discussing all things .NET MAUI, including:

- **Latest developments and the future roadmap of .NET MAUI**
- **Integration and experiences with Blazor for cross-platform applications**
- **Community questions and answers**
- **Best practices and real-world developer insights for .NET and MAUI**
- **Understanding developer tools and resources available in the .NET ecosystem**

Whether you are an experienced .NET developer or new to MAUI and Blazor, this standup offers a chance to stay updated with the latest tools, meet community leaders, and connect with Microsoft engineers.

**Featured speakers:**

- David Ortinau (@davidortinau)
- Gerald Versluis (@jfversluis)

Stay tuned for in-depth discussions, tips, and the opportunity to interact live with the .NET MAUI team and fellow developers.
