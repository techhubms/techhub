# Anders Hejlsberg on the Shift from AI Assistant to AI Agent

In this GitHub video, renowned software engineer Anders Hejlsberg explores the rapid evolution of artificial intelligence in software development. He highlights how AI is shifting from serving as a typing or code-completion assistant to becoming an autonomous agent capable of more sophisticated tasks.

## Key Topics Covered

- **AI as an Autonomous Agent**: Discussion of how modern AI is transitioning from supporting developers with simple recommendations to taking initiative in the coding process, potentially automating more of the traditional developer workflow.

- **Rethinking Developer Tools**: Hejlsberg speculates on the diminishing role of traditional IDEs as AI becomes more capable, suggesting future development environments may look fundamentally different as autonomous agents take center stage.

- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**: An introduction to the Model Context Protocol, which aims to connect various development tools, environments, and workflows, allowing AI-driven systems to access and coordinate between them efficiently.

## Notable Quotes

> "The AI is doing the work, and you're supervising."
