# AI Playbook for ROI with Microsoft Foundry | Microsoft Ignite 2025

**Speakers:** Marco Casalaina, Mike Gozzo, Nikhil Kumar, Rakhee Langer, Sethu Raman, Ian Wells  
**Session:** BRK187 | Intermediate (200)

This session provides actionable guidance on leveraging Microsoft Foundry and Azure AI for robust ROI and enterprise AI adoption. Learn practical strategies for defining, measuring, and maximizing business impact using Foundry Models, supported by real metrics and customer use cases.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Model Router Functionality**: Streamlines AI model selection and maximizes operational benefits.
- **Pricing Models & Integration**: Updated details on gated previews and integration with Azure’s standard offerings.
- **Prompt Caching**: Reduces latency and token costs for more efficient AI interactions.
- **Priority Processing**: Introducing the new hybrid model that combines PTU (Prepaid Token Units) and pay-as-you-go, optimizing for scalability and cost efficiency.
- **Cost Reduction & Capacity Planning**: Achieve up to 50% cost reduction and improved resource planning.
- **ADA (Automated Customer Service)**: Demonstrates scale for automated support using Azure AI Foundry.
- **Multi-Agent Architecture**: Delivers compliant, accurate customer service by orchestrating multiple agents for response generation.
- **Real Impact Case Studies**: Explains ROI examples such as improving efficiency for medical professionals, showing tangible business results.
- **Governance and Compliance**: Outlines strategies for responsible AI adoption and corporate governance.
- **Demo**: Ian Wells provides a walkthrough on Foundry cost control and practical deployment tips.
