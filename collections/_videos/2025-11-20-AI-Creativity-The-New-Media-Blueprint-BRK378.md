# AI + Creativity: The New Media Blueprint (BRK378)

## Overview

Explore how agentic AI is reshaping media and entertainment at Microsoft Ignite 2025.

**Speakers:** Silvia Candiani, Simon Crownshaw, Chris Petranto  
**Session Type:** Breakout | Foundational (100)  
**Language:** English (US)  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025

## Main Topics

- **Four Pillars of AI Transformation**: Discover core focus areas in deploying AI within the media industry.
- **Case Studies**: Deep dives into Kantar, Premier League, Le Monde, and Hypercinema applications highlight practical impact and industry adoption of agentic AI.
- **Kantar’s Mission**: Insights into brand building and overcoming resistance from creative agencies.
- **AI-Driven Creative Agility**: Discussion on integrating software, encouraging agility, and reimagining the human role in content creation.
- **Data Collaboration**: Examines co-engineering between Kantar and Microsoft, emphasizing smarter analytics and operational efficiency.
- **Brand Monetization with AI**: Strategies for improving media monetisation through AI-driven insights.
- **Agentic Systems**: Introduction to autonomous AI agents, automation, and the intelligence shared across technology layers.
- **Ethics & Security**: Reflections on the importance of responsible AI, security, and maintaining human ingenuity in a tech-driven future.

## Conclusions

- Integrating agentic AI throughout the media lifecycle can automate production, personalize experiences, and create new revenue streams.
- Strategic decisions on AI adoption will shape future competitiveness.
- Security, ethics, and human creativity remain foundational as AI becomes central to media workflows.
