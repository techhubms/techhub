# Solving Real MSP Challenges with Agentic AI | Microsoft Ignite 2025

**Speakers:** Brianna Berry, Jan Jofra  
**Session ID:** BRKSP451  
**Language:** English (US)  
**Level:** Intermediate (200)

## Session Overview

This session demystifies the practical use of advanced agentic AI for managed service providers (MSPs). TD SYNNEX demonstrates how real-world AI agents, not just traditional chatbots, can deliver tangible business value across service management, security, billing, automation, and more.

## Key Focus Areas

- **AI Disruption and Value Creation:** Understanding how intelligent agents differ from basic chatbots and how they are transforming MSP workflows.
- **Monetization Strategies:** Insights on building revenue streams with AI solutions, from tools like Copilot Chat to comprehensive agent-driven service offerings.
- **Partner Enablement:** Overview of StreamOne® and Destination AI™ as platforms powering AI adoption, helping partners deploy, manage, and scale AI solutions.

## Real-World Use Cases

- **Help Desk Automation:** AI agents for autonomous ticket resolution and customer support.
- **Supply Chain and Retail:** Streamlining inventory management and sales processes using agentic AI.
- **HR and Onboarding:** Personalized information delivery and workflow automation for new hires.
- **Process Integration:** Leveraging generative AI to unify disparate business operations.

## Market Research

The session is backed by proprietary research highlighting market readiness, adoption trends, and the measurable impacts of AI implementation for MSPs and their clients.

## Practical Enablement

TD SYNNEX outlines a roadmap and practical 'AI Game Plan,' introducing Gentek AI Cloud Labs for piloting and scaling these intelligent agent solutions.
