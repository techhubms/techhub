# .NET Conf 2025 - Day 1

.NET Conf returns for its 15th year as a global, virtual conference, designed to provide a world-class, engaging experience for developers regardless of their location.

## What to Expect

- Three days of live sessions covering the latest in .NET technologies.
- Presentations by expert speakers, including members of the .NET team and the broader developer community.
- Opportunities for live Q&A through platforms like Twitter and Twitch.
- Virtual attendee parties with trivia games, prizes, and networking.
- Access to a wide range of learning resources and official Microsoft platforms.

## How to Participate

- Watch live or on-demand sessions for updates, deep dives, and best practices in .NET development.
- Engage with the community on social media:
  - [Blog](https://aka.ms/dotnet/blog)
  - [Twitter](https://aka.ms/dotnet/twitter)
  - [TikTok](https://aka.ms/dotnet/tiktok)
  - [Mastodon](https://aka.ms/dotnet/mastodon)
  - [LinkedIn](https://aka.ms/dotnet/linkedin)
  - [Facebook](https://aka.ms/dotnet/facebook)
- Leverage learning resources:
  - [Docs](https://learn.microsoft.com/dotnet)
  - [Forums](https://aka.ms/dotnet/forums)
  - [Microsoft Learn](https://aka.ms/learndotnet)
  - [Q&A](https://aka.ms/dotnet-qa)

## Key Takeaways

- Enhance your knowledge and skills as a .NET developer.
- Network and interact with global peers.
- Stay updated on the latest .NET frameworks, tools, and practices.

Join in and make the most of .NET Conf 2025, learning from both product engineers and experienced community members.
