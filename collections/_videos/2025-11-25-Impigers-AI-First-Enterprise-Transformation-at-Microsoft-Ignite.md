# Impiger’s AI-First Enterprise Transformation at Microsoft Ignite

This session from Microsoft Ignite 2025 offers a candid look into Impiger’s approach to engineering enterprises for the AI era. Led by Venkat and fellow AI architects, Impiger unpacks their architecture strategy, governance practices, and platform innovations, with a focus on the Moderor.ai platform.

## Key Topics Covered

- **AI Ethics & Internal Governance**  
  - Formation of an AI Ethics Committee to guide organizational adoption and ethical standards  
  - Development of internal governance pathways for responsible AI implementation

- **AI Architecture Strategy**  
  - Overview of transformative approaches to governance using AI architecture  
  - Adoption of Zero Trust and robust guardrails for platform engineering and data security

- **Platform Engineering and LLMs**  
  - Introduction of a three-layer large language model (LLM) architecture for smarter decision-making  
  - Integration of local LLM models tailored for specialized enterprise scenarios  
  - Connection frameworks for third-party LLMs using the IMCP layer

- **Practical AI Development**  
  - Discussion of tactical approaches to AI engineering and delivery in large organizations

- **Vision and Progress**  
  - Recognition of company milestones and evolution of the shared vision for AI in the enterprise
