# Deep Dive: Extending and Customizing Aspire

.NET Aspire is designed for highly extensible, cloud-native orchestration. This session covers:

## Custom Integrations with Aspire

- Learn how to integrate proprietary services into Aspire
- Adapt Aspire for unique organizational infrastructure needs
- Extend Aspire beyond built-in capabilities to suit custom scenarios

## Extending the Dashboard

- Build your own visualizations on top of Aspire's dashboard
- Display custom telemetry and metrics relevant to your applications or operations

## Creating Custom Resource Types

- Develop resource plugins for your specific environments
- Support new cloud or on-premises infrastructure within Aspire orchestration

## Real-World Examples of Extensibility

- See demonstrations of integrating third-party or proprietary systems
- Learn patterns for extending Aspire's core logic
