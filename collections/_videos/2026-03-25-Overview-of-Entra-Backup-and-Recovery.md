# Overview of Entra Backup and Recovery

John Savill's Technical Training walks through **Microsoft Entra Backup and Restore** and how it fits alongside existing safety features like **soft delete** and **protected actions**.

## What Entra Backup and Restore adds

- **Daily snapshot** of Entra state
- Ability to **restore state** from backup
- A **difference report** to understand what changed between states
- End-to-end **recovery** flow

## How it complements other protections

- **Soft delete**
  - Used as an additional recovery mechanism for deleted items.
- **Protected actions**
  - Helps prevent or reduce the risk of destructive actions.
