# Maintain Regulatory Compliance with the Microsoft Sovereign Cloud

## Overview

This session from Microsoft Ignite 2025 examines how Microsoft Sovereign Cloud addresses a diverse array of digital sovereignty and regulatory compliance needs. Engineering leaders Harald Solstad Fianbakken and Ella Hassan discuss securing data, maintaining operational control, and meeting the demands of hybrid and regulated cloud environments through advanced compliance, security, and AI-powered solutions.

## Key Topics

- **Regulatory Landscape**: Exploration of regulatory challenges and the growing need for trusted cloud partners in handling sensitive workloads.
- **Sovereign Cloud Models**: Differentiation between Sovereign Public, Private, and Partner Cloud offerings to fit varied regulatory and data residency requirements.
- **Data Controls**: Deep dive into customer-managed encryption, advanced key management, and customer-controlled access to ensure maximum autonomy over data and operations.
- **Compliance and Auditing**: Addressing complex compliance mandates, supporting offline/cloud-disconnected scenarios, and robust auditing capabilities.
- **Private Sovereign Clouds and Azure Local**: Introduction of Azure Local and dedicated Private Sovereign Clouds to meet stringent regional and vertical sector compliance needs.
- **Security and AI Enablement**: Discussion of new innovation pillars, including enhanced security measures and the integration of AI tools to foster innovation within compliance boundaries.
- **Disaster Recovery**: Launch of new site-to-site recovery capabilities for improved resiliency and business continuity across datacenters.
- **Real-World Scenario**: Showcase of a healthcare customer using Azure Local for regulatory compliance and high availability.
