# Secure the Modern Enterprise with Varonis and Microsoft Integration

## Overview

This session from Microsoft Ignite features Brian Vecci and demonstrates the integration of Varonis with Microsoft technologies to safeguard sensitive enterprise data. The presentation addresses the challenges of managing data risks associated with the growing use of AI agents and the expansion of data across various platforms.

## Key Topics

- **Introduction to Varonis**: Discussion on Varonis as a data security company and its evolution in the enterprise landscape.
- **Data Risk in the Age of AI**: Explanation of how the proliferation of AI agents and multiple data sources increases risk for organizations.
- **Live Integration Demo**: Practical demonstration showing how Varonis integrates with Microsoft systems to:
  - Explore and protect sensitive data
  - Manage and right-size permissions
  - Monitor and reduce risk across platforms such as Salesforce
- **Data Monitoring Strategy**: Exploration of an ongoing, "land and expand" approach to enterprise data monitoring.
- **Enterprise Data Value**: Assessing the increased value and complexity of data and the importance of robust security and governance strategies.

## Session Breakdown

- Welcome and introductions
- Overview of Varonis and its Microsoft integration
- Understanding increasing data risks with AI adoption
- Live demonstration using Asset Explorer
- Data and permission management examples
- Q&A and session wrap-up

## Takeaways

- The combination of Varonis and Microsoft solutions supports organizations in securing sensitive data and enabling AI safely.
- Ongoing data monitoring and permission management are essential for reducing risk in modern enterprises.
- The integration provides actionable solutions for organizations leveraging diverse data sources and platforms.
