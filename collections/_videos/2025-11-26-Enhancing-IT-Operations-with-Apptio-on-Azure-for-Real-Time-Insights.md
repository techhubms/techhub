# Enhancing IT Operations with Apptio on Azure for Real-Time Insights

## Session Overview

This Microsoft Ignite 2025 session discusses challenges faced by enterprises in managing multi-cloud environments and distributed IT resources. The speakers, Philip Alfano and Bill Primerano, outline how Apptio integrated on Azure empowers joint clients with actionable, real-time insights, enabling teams to forecast spend and make data-driven decisions for streamlining operations and reducing risks.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Multi-Cloud Complexity**: Understanding the impact of managing thousands of resources across diverse platforms.
- **Operational Challenges**: Identifying inefficiencies and limited visibility within distributed teams.
- **Apptio on Azure**: Leveraging this integration for:
  - Real-time operational insights
  - AI-powered optimization of resources
  - Financial transparency and spend forecasting
- **Competitive Advantage**: Translating operational efficiency into business value and cost savings for enterprises.

## Solutions Discussed

- Use of AI-based tools for continuous optimization
- Real-time dashboards and analytics for financial management
- Centralized visibility to streamline cross-team operations
- Techniques for uncovering hidden savings within IT estates
