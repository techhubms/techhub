**Panelists:** Gwyneth Peña-Siguenza, Marlene Mhangami, Pamela Fox, Sara Kaiser, Renee Noble, Anthony Shaw

Join the Python on Azure team as they reflect on 2025’s key Python trends, share valuable learning resources, and discuss strategies for ongoing professional growth. The conversation also covers highlights in AI, common challenges, and emerging opportunities for 2026.
