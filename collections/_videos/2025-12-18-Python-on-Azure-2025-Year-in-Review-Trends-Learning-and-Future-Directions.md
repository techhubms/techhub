# Python on Azure: 2025 Year in Review – Trends, Learning, and Future Directions

**Panelists:** Gwyneth Peña-Siguenza, Marlene Mhangami, Pamela Fox, Sara Kaiser, Renee Noble, Anthony Shaw
**Hosted by:** Microsoft Developer

## Overview

Join the Python on Azure team as they reflect on 2025’s key Python trends, share valuable learning resources, and discuss strategies for ongoing professional growth. The conversation also covers highlights in AI, common challenges, and emerging opportunities for 2026.

---

## Topics Covered

- **Favorite Python Trends of 2025:**
  - Key changes and improvements in the Python language and ecosystem.
  - Adoption of new Python features and libraries.
- **Learning Resources:**
  - Books, websites, and tutorials that made a difference this year (`[see extended resource links below]`).
- **How the Team Learns:**
  - Practical strategies for staying current with new developments.
  - Balancing foundational skill-building with exploring new paradigms.
- **AI “Wins” for 2025:**
  - Major advancements and success stories for AI in Python and Azure contexts.
- **Biggest Hurdles:**
  - Discussing challenges and solutions that arose when working with Python and related tech.
- **Looking Towards 2026:**
  - Topics and technologies the group is excited to explore in the coming year.
