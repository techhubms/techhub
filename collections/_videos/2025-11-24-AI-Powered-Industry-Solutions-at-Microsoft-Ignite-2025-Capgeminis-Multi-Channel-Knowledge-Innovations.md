# AI-Powered Industry Solutions at Microsoft Ignite 2025

**Presented by: Microsoft Events**

This session from Microsoft Ignite 2025 explores collaborative innovations between Microsoft technologies and Capgemini’s industry expertise, with a strong focus on AI and operational scale.

## Session Highlights

- **Welcome and Introduction**
  - Overview of Microsoft Ignite’s focus on transformative technology.

- **AI Transformation for Efficiency**
  - Capgemini shares strategies for leveraging AI to boost customer efficiency.

- **Multi-Channel Dynamic Knowledge Creation**
  - Introduction to building knowledge systems that span multiple channels using AI.
  - Emphasis on combining Microsoft platforms with Capgemini’s domain expertise for scalable, intelligent solutions.

- **Intelligent Operation Centers**
  - Success story of supporting over 3 million users within an intelligent operation center.
  - Insights on technology choices and scale.

- **Voice Technology for Support Expansion**
  - How voice technology enhances support capabilities and operational responsiveness.

- **Domain Knowledge Integration**
  - Strategies for embedding domain expertise into GitHub and internal platforms for organization-wide impact.

- **Agentic Industry Studio Impact**
  - Discussion of cross-platform impact and innovations introduced by agentic industry studio across Microsoft technologies.

## Key Technologies and Concepts

- Microsoft AI platforms (unspecified but referenced in knowledge creation, operations, and support contexts)
- GitHub integrations for domain knowledge sharing
- Voice technology as a support tool
- Industry-scale operations with multi-channel engagement

## Practical Insights

- Strategies to build dynamic knowledge networks using Microsoft AI
- Integrating organizational skills and domain expertise into developer tooling like GitHub
- Scaling AI-powered support systems across millions of users
- Operationalizing AI for tangible business and industry benefits
