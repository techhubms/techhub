# Optimizing Cloud and AI ROI with Microsoft Marketplace Strategies

**Speakers:** Matt Hillegass, Rajesh Ramankutty  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 (Session BRK215)

## Session Overview

This session targets organizations looking to accelerate AI-driven innovation through optimized cloud strategies. The focus is on leveraging the Microsoft Marketplace to balance performance, scalability, and cost control while ensuring governance and compliance.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Growth of Cloud Marketplaces & Digital Transformation**  
  Examination of industry trends emphasizing the move towards cloud marketplace solutions for digital transformation, speeding up AI adoption.

- **Partner Tool Integration**  
  Examples include tools like Claysar and User Lane, helping map cloud applications to MAC eligible lists for streamlined solution tracking.

- **Procurement Enhancements**  
  Introduction of a new Purchase Order (PO) Management Service that enables detailed mapping of cloud spend to accounting and procurement systems, improving financial oversight.

- **Enabling Departmental Collaboration and AI Solution Sourcing**  
  Case studies (such as Mars) illustrate how Marketplace adds value by facilitating collaboration across departments and accelerating AI solution discovery and onboarding.

- **Contract Management and Procurement Templates**  
  Strategies to reduce late-stage contract friction and speed up renewals. Includes the rollout of pre-approved governance and vendor onboarding templates.

- **Cross-Functional Teams for Innovation**  
  Practical guidance on building integrated teams that can deliver solutions faster using Marketplace provisions and governance frameworks.

- **Azure Adoption Pathways**  
  Recommendations tailored to customers at various stages of Azure cloud adoption, emphasizing best practices in Marketplace utilization to optimize ROI.

## Takeaways

- Use Microsoft Marketplace to accelerate deployment of compliant, scalable AI and cloud solutions
- Leverage pre-integrated partner tools for efficient application mapping and procurement
- Adopt new procurement and governance services to streamline accounting and vendor management
- Build cross-functional teams to enhance adoption speed and innovation

## Conclusion

This session provides a blueprint for leaders and technical practitioners to enhance their cloud and AI investment returns by aligning business goals, technical innovation, and operational discipline—utilizing resources and services available through the Microsoft Marketplace.
