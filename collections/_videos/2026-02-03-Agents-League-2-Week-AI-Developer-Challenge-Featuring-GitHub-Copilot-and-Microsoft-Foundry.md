# Agents League: 2-Week AI Developer Challenge (Feb 16-27)

**Build, Battle, and Level Up.**

Agents League is a unique 2-week developer challenge created for those looking to push their AI engineering skills alongside top MVPs, Microsoft product teams, and the broader developer community. Participants can join live coding battles or simply follow along while learning about AI agent development across several innovative tracks.

## Challenge Tracks

- **Creative Apps**: Use GitHub Copilot to accelerate app development and creativity.
- **Reasoning Agents**: Tackle complex problems and agent design with Microsoft Foundry.
- **Enterprise Agents**: Explore building productivity solutions with agents in Microsoft 365 Copilot.

## What to Expect

- Live streamed events with real-time build battles
- Collaboration and competition between MVPs, product teams, and the community
- Focus on hands-on AI agent development using industry-leading Microsoft technologies

## How to Join

- Open to anyone interested in AI development, from beginners to seasoned pros
- Participate in any of the three tracks or watch the action unfold live
- **Register here**: [aka.ms/agentsleague/register](https://aka.ms/agentsleague/register)

## Key Technologies Featured

- **GitHub Copilot**: For code-assisted app creation
- **Microsoft Foundry**: For advanced reasoning agents
- **Azure**: Underpinning cloud infrastructure and AI services

Upgrade your skills, connect with experts, and experience the combined power of Microsoft’s AI development ecosystem in action.
