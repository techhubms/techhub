# Live Platform: Cloud-Based Unified Communications with Microsoft Teams Integration

## Overview

AudioCodes Live Platform, showcased at Microsoft Ignite 2025, is designed to deliver unified communications and contact center services in the cloud. Leveraging certified solutions, AI innovation, and Microsoft Teams, it streamlines both employee (EX) and customer (CX) experiences.

### Key Features

- **Automated Onboarding**: Simplifies initial setup for partners and organizations.
- **Seamless PSTN Connectivity**: Integrates with Microsoft Teams, Webex Calling, and Zoom Phone.
- **Centralized Management & Compliance**: Provides comprehensive management tools and supports regulatory compliance.
- **AI-Powered Voice Applications**: Enhances interactions with Meeting Insights, Interaction Insights, and other AI-enabled services.
- **Unified Platform Concept**: Centralizes resources for easier provisioning and service management.

### Microsoft Integration Highlights

- Deep focus on provisioning across cyber platforms with Microsoft at the core.
- Day-two management and value-added service offerings tailored to Microsoft Teams environments.
- Overview of Microsoft NDA and agreement requirements for certified partners.
