# .NET MAUI Community Standup - .NET 10 Announcements Roundup

Join David Ortinau and Gerald Versluis as they review the newest features and changes brought by .NET 10, with a focus on the .NET MAUI (Multi-platform App UI) framework. This community standup presents a summary of what has shipped in the past month, including:

- Major improvements and new features in .NET 10
- Updates to the .NET MAUI development experience
- Community contributions and third-party libraries
- Demos and tips for cross-platform development
