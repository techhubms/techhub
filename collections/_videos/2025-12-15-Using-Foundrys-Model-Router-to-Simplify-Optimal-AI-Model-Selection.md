# Using Foundry's Model Router to Simplify Optimal AI Model Selection

**Author:** John Savill's Technical Training

## Overview

This video provides a step-by-step walkthrough of Microsoft Foundry's Model Router, aimed at simplifying the selection and deployment of the most optimal AI models in your Azure-based AI applications and agents.

### Key Topics Covered

- **Model Dimensions:** Understand various characteristics to consider when evaluating AI and language models (LLMs, SLMs) for your solutions.
- **Picking the Right Model:** Learn how to compare and select models based on relevant criteria for your use case.
- **Model Router Overview:** An introduction to how the Model Router service works within Microsoft Azure Foundry.
- **Routing Methods:** Explore different strategies and options available for routing requests between models, enabling flexibility and optimization.
- **Deployment Steps:** Guidance on deploying and configuring the Model Router in Azure, including backend model integration.
- **How Everything Fits Together:** Visual explanation of the model selection and routing lifecycle.
- **Hands-On Demo:** A practical demonstration of integrating and testing the Model Router with your own AI agents and applications.
