# Drive Innovation with AI, Hybrid Cloud, and Azure Storage Solutions

This intermediate-level Microsoft Ignite 2025 session spotlights the evolving partnership between Dell Technologies and Microsoft, demonstrating how they leverage AI technologies and cloud infrastructure to enhance enterprise workflows and efficiency.

## Session Highlights

- **Hybrid Cloud Transformation**: Overview of the Dell and Microsoft partnership, focusing on adapting to rapid changes in IT and cloud management.
- **Dell AI Factory and Copilot+**: Explore Dell AI Factory benefits and experience Copilot+ in Dell AI PCs to support dynamic workflow automation and productivity in business environments.
- **Azure Local and PowerStore Integration**:
  - Explanation of the disaggregated storage capability provided by Azure Local, enabling flexibility and control for private cloud infrastructure.
  - Support for more than 20,000 PowerStore customers in easing migration to Azure Local while maximizing cost savings through reuse of existing storage investments.
  - Demonstration of cost advantages, with solutions supporting over 8 petabytes of data in a single namespace.
- **Enterprise Storage with PowerScale for Azure**: Introduction to PowerScale integration within Azure, providing scalable, reliable, and robust enterprise storage tailored for complex, modern workloads.
- **Cloud Efficiency and Control**: How Azure Local reimagines private cloud infrastructure, emphasizing efficiency, control, and streamlined management for organizations.
- **Future Collaboration**: Ongoing efforts and vision for future collaboration between Dell Technologies and Microsoft in enterprise cloud and AI innovation.

## Key Concepts

- **Scalability and Reliability**: Emphasized through PowerScale's enterprise-grade storage solutions and Azure's ability to leverage high-volume workloads.
- **Migration and Cost Efficiency**: Supporting existing Dell PowerStore customers with easier migration to Azure Local, reducing costs by repurposing hardware.

## Conclusion

This session serves as a practical overview for IT professionals and consultants interested in combining AI capabilities, hybrid cloud architectures, and next-generation storage solutions powered by Dell and Microsoft Azure.
