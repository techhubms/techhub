# Leading with Trust: Building & Deploying Agents in a Regulated World

## Session Overview

This intermediate session from Microsoft Ignite 2025 highlights strategies for aligning with global AI and cybersecurity regulations using Microsoft cloud services and compliance solutions. The focus is on enabling organizations to achieve regulatory resilience and trusted innovation when building and deploying AI agents.

## Key Topics Covered

- Growth of agent adoption and the rising importance of compliance.
- Building compliance into the AI agent lifecycle, with practical guidance and demo scenarios.
- Automated compliance evaluations in Microsoft Purview for Foundry agents.
- Implementing organization-wide communication compliance policies for real-time safety.
- Continuous compliance monitoring and maintenance, demonstrated live.
- Launching AI-powered regulatory templates for expedited rule alignment.
- Creating and applying assessments for new regulations to Foundry projects.
- Identifying and resolving high-risk actions during agent evaluation.
- Post-deployment guardrails and multi-layer security analogies for robust protection.

## Featured Solutions

- **Microsoft Purview Compliance Manager:** Automate compliance checks and monitor regulatory alignment.
- **Regulatory Navigator:** Track and apply international regulations (EU AI Act, NIS2, DORA, DPDP India).
- **Defender for Cloud:** Monitor cloud posture and security controls in real time.

## Responsible AI & Regulatory Resilience

The session embeds Responsible AI principles throughout the agent lifecycle, ensuring that regulatory requirements not only strengthen cybersecurity but also foster trusted innovation in AI solutions.
