# Engineering the Future in the Age of Digital Product Innovation

**Session Type:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 Breakout (Intermediate)
**Speakers:** Simon Floyd, Dennis Goetting, Heather Kerrick, Alfonso Rodriguez

## Overview

Explore how Microsoft, together with partners like Autodesk and PTC, is transforming industrial engineering through:

- **AI-driven product configuration and simulation**
- **Cloud automation using Azure and GitHub**
- **Robotics and autonomous systems for scalable automation**
- **Agentic AI powering PLM and ERP modernization**

## Key Topics & Chapters

- **Frontier Firm & Industrial Frontier Concepts:** Setting a vision for next-gen digital engineering.
- **AI in Product Configuration & Robotics:** Real customer examples of measurable value through automation and smarter workflows.
- **Vault Search and Fusion Cloud Data Automation with MCP Integration:** Demonstrating seamless enterprise data management.
- **Autodesk + Microsoft Collaboration:** Leveraging AI-generated imagery and dynamic presentations for design innovation.
- **PLM and ERP Integration Using AI Agents:** Streamlining cross-team communication and reducing operational delays.
- **Demo Conclusion:** Showcasing gains in speed, cost savings, and innovation.
- **Recap and Vision:** Using AI agents to automate workflows, augment teams, and foster ongoing industry collaboration.

## Technical Highlights

- Use of **Azure** as foundational cloud platform for scalable automation and AI services.
- Integration with **GitHub** for DevOps lifecycle management.
- Application of **agentic AI** for workflow optimization, simulation, and data-driven decision making.
- Collaboration with **Autodesk** for enhanced, AI-powered design tools.
