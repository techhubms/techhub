# Transform Manufacturing with Digital Twins and Real-Time Simulation

## Overview

This session, part of Microsoft Ignite 2025, features experts from NVIDIA, Microsoft, and Ansys (now Synopsys) discussing how advanced simulation technologies and digital twins are transforming the manufacturing sector.

### Key Topics Covered

- **Leveraging Digital Twins**: Manufacturers utilize digital twins for virtual modeling of plants and equipment, enabling rapid scenario testing and performance optimization.
- **Real-Time Simulation**: NVIDIA AI and Omniverse libraries, integrated with Microsoft Azure, deliver powerful, scalable real-time simulations for manufacturing operations and traffic data analytics.
- **Accelerated Time to Insight**: By using Azure cloud and NVIDIA DeepStream, organizations can quickly deploy trained AI models, analyze live city traffic data, and visualize outcomes on interactive 3D maps.
- **Operational Optimization**: Innovations include optimizing signal timing for roadways, improving emergency responses through real-time incident detection, and future-proofing infrastructure design.
- **Collaboration and Learning**: The session highlights active partnerships among Microsoft, NVIDIA, and Ansys, showcasing valuable lessons learned from real-world AI traffic projects.
