# Blazor Community Standup: Blazor validation in .NET 11

In this Blazor Community Standup episode, **dotnet** (featuring **Daniel Roth** and **Ondřej Roztočil**) looks at upcoming validation improvements in **Blazor** for **.NET 11**.

## What’s covered

- **Async validation**
  - Validation workflows that can run asynchronously.
- **Localized validation messages**
  - Support for presenting validation feedback in localized languages.
- **Client-side validation without requiring an interactive render mode**
  - Validation scenarios that work client-side without depending on an interactive render mode.
