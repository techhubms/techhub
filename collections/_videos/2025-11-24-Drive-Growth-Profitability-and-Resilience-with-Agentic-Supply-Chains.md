# Drive Growth, Profitability and Resilience with Agentic Supply Chains

## Session Overview

This breakout session from Microsoft Ignite 2025 dives into how supply chains are shifting from reactive processes to intelligent, AI-powered networks. Experts from Microsoft, Cummins, and Blue Yonder discuss real-world examples highlighting the impact of agentic supply chains on business growth, profitability, and resilience.

### Key Topics

- **Unifying Supply Chain Data Platforms**: Transitioning from siloed systems to integrated, centralized data platforms leveraging Microsoft technologies such as Microsoft Fabric and Azure AI.
- **AI Agents in Operations**: Deployment of AI agents for real-time logistics optimization, predictive insights, and automated orchestration across planning and fulfillment workflows.
- **Collaboration Case Study**: Insights into the Cummins and Blue Yonder partnership, demonstrating modernization of supply chain systems for global operations.
- **Sustainability and Reverse Logistics**: Strategies for optimizing logistics, handling returns, and enhancing cost analysis through automation and advanced analytics.
- **Synchronized Operations**: Discussion of the journey and solutions required to move supply chains from fragmented, manual processes to synchronized, AI-driven networks.

## Speaker Contributions

- **Chris Burchett**: Outlined the technical architecture for unifying supply chain data and integrating AI capabilities.
- **Armando Duran**: Shared Cummins’ mission-driven approach and real-world experience in supply chain modernization using Microsoft tools.
- **Yury Gomez**: Explored manufacturing and operational outcomes, focusing on data-driven and autonomous supply networks.

## Featured Microsoft Technologies and Solutions

- **Microsoft Fabric**: Data engineering platform for centralizing and synchronizing supply chain data.
- **Azure AI and Machine Learning Services**: Offer real-time forecasting, predictive analysis, and intelligent workflow automation.
- **Agentic Supply Chain Concepts**: Use of autonomous, AI-powered software agents to self-orchestrate supply chain processes.
