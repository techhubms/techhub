# Integrating Copilot Studio and Azure AI Foundry for Accelerated AI Development

**Speaker:** Matt DiLiberto
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025

This session explores how Copilot Studio, a low-code agent builder, and Azure AI Foundry, a suite for AI governance and model fine-tuning, work together to support every stage of the AI development lifecycle. Real-world integration strategies and use cases are discussed, illustrating how these Microsoft technologies enable rapid development of intelligent and scalable business solutions.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Copilot Studio Overview**
  - Introduction to building low-code AI agents.
  - Empowerment of teams with easy agent orchestration and collaboration features.

- **Agent-to-Agent Orchestration**
  - Evolution of agent collaboration using Microsoft tools.
  - Coordination strategies for scalable multi-agent solutions.

- **Live Demo Highlights**
  - Activity Explorer usage for reviewing completed Statements of Work (SOW) steps.
  - Real demo showcasing transition from concept to implementation.

- **Azure AI Foundry Features**
  - Testing, fine-tuning, and governance within the Azure AI ecosystem.
  - Utilization of fine-tuned models with custom prompt tools.
  - Structuring AI responses with topics for deterministic results.

- **Integration Strategies**
  - How combining low-code agent development with robust governance frameworks enables more reliable and scalable solutions.
  - Best practices for integrating Copilot Studio and Azure AI Foundry in real-world projects.
