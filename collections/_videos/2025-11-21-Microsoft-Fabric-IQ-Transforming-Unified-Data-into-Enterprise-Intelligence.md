# Microsoft Fabric IQ: Transforming Unified Data into Enterprise Intelligence

## Overview

Microsoft Fabric IQ, unveiled at Ignite 2025, showcases the transformation of enterprise data into a live semantic model. The unified platform empowers teams and AI agents to collaborate using shared entity, relationship, and rule definitions, driving real-time insights, autonomous operations, and smarter business decisions.

### Key Features Highlighted

- **Power Ontology & Integration:** Explanation of power ontology’s role in integrating dispersed datasets for grid analysis across business domains.
- **Semantic Modeling:** Structured representation of entities, relationships, and rules—enabling common understanding and automation.
- **AI & Agent Collaboration:** Teams and AI agents converge on unified data models, leveraging reasoning and rule automation for operational alerts and recommendations.
- **Automated Operational Actions:** Ontology-driven automation for real-time response in use cases such as airport runway management and data center operations.
- **Geospatial and Insight Generation:** Utilizing ontological models for advanced geospatial analysis and insight generation.
- **Operations Agent Demo:** Integration with Microsoft Teams demonstrates alerting, recommendations, and reasoning capabilities that extend enterprise operations.
- **AI Digital Trust:** Kyndryl’s Agentic AI shows applied digital trust for data center operations.

## Demonstrations & Scenarios

- **Business Ontology Integration:** Example integration for airport runway task management, blending data, rules, and real-time AI guidance.
- **Operational Intelligence via Teams:** Operations Agent leverages Teams for workflow automation, alerting, and insight delivery.
- **Rule-Based Automation:** Automated rules triggered by semantic entity relationships for operational efficiency.
- **Geospatial Insights:** Mapping and grid analysis across multiple business domains using unified ontologies.
- **Third-Party Integration:** Kyndryl showcases digital trust and AI-driven operations within the Fabric IQ platform.
