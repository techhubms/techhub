# Azure Local LENS Workbook: Fleet-Level Insights at a Glance

As Azure Local environments expand, the challenges of managing distributed infrastructure shift from individual clusters to the entire fleet. Troubleshooting a single cluster is not enough at scale—you need holistic, fleet-level visibility. The Azure Local LENS Workbook provides exactly that:

## What is Azure Local LENS?

Azure Local LENS (Lifecycle, Events & Notification Status) is a **community-driven, open-source Azure Monitor Workbook** that delivers deep insights into the health, compliance, and trends of extensive Azure Local environments. It's designed to enable architects and operations teams to:

- Visualize the state of all Azure Local clusters in a single view
- Quickly assess connection, health, and update status across the fleet
- Detect recurring issues and outliers
- Track workload and VM deployment trends
- Streamline compliance and update readiness

Instead of checking resources one by one, operators use the workbook's standardized dashboards and drill-down workflows to identify problems and opportunities across many sites.

## Key Features

- **Fleet Overview:** Summary of all clusters, their health and connectivity
- **Workload Trends:** Monitor the distribution and growth of VMs and AKS Arc clusters on Azure Local
- **Health & Compliance:** Identify persistent problems and update compliance
- **Drill-Downs:** Move seamlessly from a high-level view to specific clusters or resources
- **Operational Consistency:** Shared visuals and metrics for teams to align on priorities

## Community-Driven and Open Source

Hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/Azure/AzureLocal-LENS-Workbook), Azure Local LENS welcomes contributions and feedback. The repository provides:

- The full JSON workbook
- Release notes
- Contribution guidelines
- Issue tracking

*Note: Azure Local LENS is not an official Microsoft product, but an openly developed community project built for real-world hybrid cloud operations.*

## Why Does This Matter?

For central operations teams, architects, and platform engineers, Azure Local LENS addresses the operational demands of large estates:

- **Proactive Fleet Health Reviews**
- **Update Planning and Compliance Monitoring**
- **Systemic Issue Detection**
- **Capacity and Workload Trend Analysis**

In short, the workbook transforms raw monitoring data into actionable, decision-ready insights, promoting operational excellence and agility.
