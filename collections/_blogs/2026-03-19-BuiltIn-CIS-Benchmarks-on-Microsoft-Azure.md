# Built‑In CIS Benchmarks on Microsoft Azure

Security and compliance are often well-defined on paper—but applying them consistently across real-world environments is where things get challenging, especially in **hybrid and regulated scenarios**.

Thomas Maurer describes a conversation with **Pal Lakatos‑Toth** (Senior Product Manager at Microsoft) about **built-in CIS Benchmarks on Azure** and how Microsoft is helping organizations move **from policy to practice**. The discussion focuses on how CIS Benchmarks are becoming more **platform-integrated, flexible, and hybrid-ready**, rather than remaining static checklists.

## Why built-in CIS Benchmarks on Azure matter

Built-in CIS Benchmarks on Azure are positioned as a way to apply **standardized security baselines** while still allowing operational flexibility needed in modern:

- Cloud environments
- **Hybrid** setups
- Edge scenarios
- **Sovereign** and **highly regulated** contexts

The goal is to support real operational needs—not only audit requirements—where **consistency, transparency, and control** are critical.
