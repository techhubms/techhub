# Migrate & Modernize Your VMware Platform Using Azure VMware Solution Gen 2

**Authors:** Carlos Villuendas and Trevor Davis (Microsoft Global Blackbelts)

This ongoing video series offers step-by-step guidance for organizations looking to migrate their on-premises VMware workloads to Azure VMware Solution (AVS) Gen 2. The content starts with configuring prerequisites, covers workload deployment and migration, and demonstrates how to integrate migrated VMware environments with native Azure services to unlock further value.

## Video Lineup

**1. [Prerequisites](https://youtu.be/xQaA0NBX6eo):**

- Walkthrough of necessary configuration steps prior to migration
- Covers network, identity, and access requirements

**2. [Deployment](https://youtu.be/DHEmxR-PD2o):**

- Demonstrates the deployment process of VMware workloads onto AVS
- Shows migration considerations, configuration, and initial integration

## Series Highlights

- Best practices for hybrid cloud architecture
- How to leverage Azure-native tools and services post-migration
- Support for continuous improvement through additional upcoming episodes
