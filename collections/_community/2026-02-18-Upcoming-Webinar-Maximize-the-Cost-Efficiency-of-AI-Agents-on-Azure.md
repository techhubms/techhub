# Maximize the Cost Efficiency of AI Agents on Azure: Webinar Overview

As AI agents take center stage in automating enterprise workflows and driving innovation, organizations face increasing questions about cost, ROI, and sustainable scaling. This free webinar provides a pragmatic, technical roadmap for building and deploying AI agents on Azure responsibly and efficiently.

## What You'll Learn

- Identify high-impact AI agent use cases on Azure
- Connect architectural decisions (model choice, scaling patterns) with cost drivers
- Forecast ROI and evaluate long-term sustainability of AI initiatives
- Avoid common pitfalls in AI spend as you expand across the enterprise
- Implement ongoing optimization and governance for AI projects

## Who Should Attend?

- Developers
- Administrators
- Solution Architects
- AI Engineers
- Business Analysts
- Business Users
- Technology Managers

The material targets technical practitioners and decision makers seeking to balance innovation with financial discipline in Azure AI deployments.

## Webinar Highlights

- Real-world examples illustrating cost strategies for Azure-based AI agents
- Frameworks for aligning technical AI approaches with measurable business outcomes
- Live Q&A to address implementation challenges and ROI measurement
- Complementary Microsoft Learn training for further self-paced study
