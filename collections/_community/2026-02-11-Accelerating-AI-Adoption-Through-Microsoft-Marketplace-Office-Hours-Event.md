# Accelerating AI Adoption Through Microsoft Marketplace: Office Hours Event

Microsoft Marketplace offers a wide range of prebuilt AI applications, agents, and solutions to help organizations bridge the gap between AI experimentation and meaningful business impact. This community-driven office hours event is designed to:  

- Showcase practical AI use cases available on the Microsoft Marketplace
- Explain how these AI solutions simplify purchasing, deployment, and integration with your existing Microsoft investments
- Highlight real-world business scenarios and best practices that drive successful AI adoption in the enterprise

## Event Details

- **Frequency:** Last Wednesday of every month (8:30 AM Pacific Time)
- **Format:** Open office hours, no registration required — simply add the event to your calendar and join via the Tech Community platform
- **Interaction:** Ask questions before or during the live session to get personalized advice on the best ways to accelerate your AI journey

## Key Topics Covered

- Types of AI use cases available through the Marketplace (prebuilt apps, agents, industry solutions)
- Deployment and integration workflows tailored for Microsoft cloud environments
- Guidance on maximizing the performance and value of your Microsoft cloud investments with AI
- Best practices to overcome common roadblocks during AI evaluation, purchase, and implementation

## How to Join

1. Add the scheduled event to your calendar.
2. Sign into the Tech Community portal.
3. Select "Attend" to enable reminders and participate live.
4. Engage with Microsoft experts by posting questions in advance or during the broadcast.

Take advantage of ongoing support by attending the recurring office hours to stay up-to-date with new AI marketplace solutions and strategies for accelerating enterprise AI adoption.
