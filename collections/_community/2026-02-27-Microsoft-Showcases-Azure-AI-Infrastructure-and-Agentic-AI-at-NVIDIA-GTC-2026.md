# Microsoft at NVIDIA GTC 2026: Powering the Future of Scalable AI with Azure

Microsoft returns to NVIDIA GTC 2026 in San Jose, presenting a robust lineup of technical sessions, live demos, and in-depth discussions on cutting-edge AI infrastructure. The focus is on how Azure AI infrastructure enables organizations to train, infer, and deploy AI solutions at global scale in partnership with NVIDIA and industry leaders.

## Exclusive Event Experiences

- **LEGO® Datacenter Model**: Hands-on exploration of Azure AI infrastructure at the Park Container.
- **Candy Lounge & Outdoor Juice Truck**: Social networking, co-branded treats, and refreshments.
- **Networking Lounge**: Casual meetups and device charging zones.

## Key Sponsored Breakout Sessions

### Reinventing Semiconductor Design with Microsoft Discovery

- Speaker: Prashant Varshney (Microsoft)
- Overview: Leveraging Microsoft Discovery AI for Science and Synopsys Agent Engineers to automate electronic design automation (EDA) with an agentic approach on Azure.

### Operationalizing Agentic AI at Hyperscale

- Speakers: Nitin Nagarkatte, Anand Raman, Vipul Modi (Microsoft)
- Overview: Deploying and coordinating intelligent agents at enterprise scale using Azure as the backbone and Microsoft Foundry as control plane.
