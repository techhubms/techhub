# Plan Your Microsoft Ignite Experience Around the Topics that Matter Most to You

## Get Ready for Microsoft Ignite 2025

Welcome to the Azure Tech Community's roundup of recommended sessions for developers attending Microsoft Ignite 2025. This year’s event brings hundreds of sessions covering Azure, AI, DevOps, and innovation topics tailored to help developers modernize their skillset and technical strategy.

### Top Recommended Sessions for Developers

To help developers navigate Ignite, here are some top picks focused on building, deploying, and innovating using Azure and AI:

1. **[Reimagining Software Development with GitHub Copilot and AI Agents (BRK105)](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/BRK105?source=sessions)**
   - Discover how AI is transforming development workflows, from code writing to pull requests, and enhancing team productivity.
2. **[Build AI Apps Fast with GitHub and Azure AI Foundry (BRK110)](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/BRK110?source=sessions)**
   - Explore end-to-end development, training, and deployment of intelligent apps on GitHub and Azure AI Foundry.
3. **[Building and Deploying Data Agents in Microsoft Fabric (THR738)](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/THR738)**
   - Learn best practices for managing enterprise data agents using Microsoft Fabric, CI/CD, and scalable AI integrations.
4. **[CI/CD for Fabric: Accelerating Lakehouse to Production in 25 Minutes (THR739)](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/THR739?source=sessions)**
   - Get practical with Git-powered deployment, parameterization, and automation to confidently move Lakehouse projects to production.
5. **[Build A2A and MCP Systems Using SWE Agents and agent-framework (LAB513)](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/c2b74e85-85ef-4a12-92f3-45c9234db913)**
   - Hands-on lab for building, orchestrating, and deploying multi-agent systems with the latest agent technology and frameworks.

### Key Moments and Must-Attend Sessions

Before attending specialized sessions, prioritize these essential Ignite moments:

- **[Opening Keynote (KEY01)](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/KEY01?source=sessions)**
  - Hear the latest on AI, Microsoft Cloud, and developer innovation from Microsoft leaders.
- **[Innovation Session: Your AI Apps and Agent Factory (BRK1706)](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/BRK1706?source=sessions)**
  - Learn about core AI capabilities, Copilot experiences, and responsible AI frameworks empowering developers.
- **[Innovation Session: Agents at Work (BRK1708)](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions?filter=&search=BRK1708&sortBy=relevance)**
  - See real-world examples of AI agents in Microsoft 365, Dynamics, and Azure ecosystems.

### Making the Most of Ignite

- Use the [session scheduler](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions) to filter, personalize, and plan your Ignite agenda.
- Take advantage of networking, community events, and direct engagement with other Azure and AI professionals.
- Share your experience with #MSIgnite and #AzureTechCommunity, and stay updated via the [Azure Tech Community](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/category/Azure).

## Conclusion

With thoughtful planning and a focus on these highly relevant sessions, you'll maximize your learning and inspiration at Microsoft Ignite 2025. Explore leading-edge technology, connect with peers, and drive your developer journey forward.
