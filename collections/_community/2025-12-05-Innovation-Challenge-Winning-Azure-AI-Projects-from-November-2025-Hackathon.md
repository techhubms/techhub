# Innovation Challenge: Winning Azure AI Projects from November 2025 Hackathon

Every year, the Innovation Challenge program showcases the power and creativity of diverse developer teams building AI solutions using Azure. The November 2025 hackathon saw over 300 participants tackling real-world use cases and delivering 76 projects leveraging Microsoft's AI and cloud technologies.

## Hackathon Focus Areas

Teams were asked to solve practical problems using Azure AI capabilities:

- **Auto-resolve Service Desk:** Multi-agent AI service desk to reduce wait times and automate ticket handling.
- **Civic Chat:** Civic engagement platforms for accessible government info, personalized updates, and community discussion—all powered by Azure AI services.
- **Customer Personalization Orchestrator:** AI-driven systems to segment users, automate content creation, and orchestrate A/B testing with built-in safety measures.

## Award-Winning Projects

**First Place ($10,000):**

- [AgroHelpdesk](https://github.com/AgroHelpdesk/HackathonMicrosoftNov2025): Intelligent agribusiness service desk using a coordinated set of AI agents and automation.

**Second Place ($5,000):**

- [CivicUtopia](https://github.com/SlugVortex/Civic-Utopia): Civic engagement platform leveraging Azure AI for government interaction and political participation.
- [Multi-Agent Service Desk for Education](https://github.com/df478/Opticloud2): AI system resolving routine educational service desk issues, escalating only complex cases to humans.

**Third Place ($2,500):**

- [ResolveIQ](https://github.com/seojedaperez/InnovationChallenge2025-CodigoFacilito-ITDevelopers-ResolveIQ): Autonomous helpdesk AI orchestration using Azure cognitive services for smarter customer support.
- [ChainReach AI](https://innovationstudio.microsoft.com/hackathons/Innovation-Challenge-November-2025/project/112100): Multichannel marketing personalization driven by AI.
- [CivicChat (D.C.)](https://github.com/CivicChat/CivicChat): Multilingual AI civic engagement assistant for government transparency and community empowerment.
