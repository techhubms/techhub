# The March 2026 Innovation Challenge Winners

For this round of the Innovation Challenge, the sponsored organizations helped **15,000+ developers** gain skills to build **AI solutions on Azure**. The program also supported:

- Nearly **1,000** people earning **Microsoft certifications** and **Applied Skills** credentials
- **300** participants in the invitation-only **March 2026 Innovation Challenge hackathon**

Teams represented:

- [SHPE](https://shpe.org/)
- [Women in Cloud](https://womenincloud.com/aichallenge/)
- [Código Facilito](https://codigofacilito.com/)
- [DIO](https://www.dio.me/en)
- [GenSpark](https://genspark.net/)
- [NASA Space Apps](https://www.spaceappschallenge.org/nasa-space-apps-2024/2024-local-events/chicago-il/)
- [Project Blue Mountain](https://www.youtube.com/@projectbluemountainacademia)
- [TechBridge](https://techbridge.org/)

## Winning projects

## First place ($10,000)

[Pebble. - AI Cognitive Load Companion](https://github.com/dfig777/Cognitive-Load---SHPE-2026-Hackathon)

- An AI cognitive support companion aimed at turning overwhelming documents, tasks, and information into calm, structured clarity
- Framed as support for neurodivergent minds, but intended to be useful more broadly

## Second place ($5,000)

[The Living Memory Bridge](https://github.com/n0va-ctrl/memory-bridge)

- Focused on dementia as an extreme form of cognitive overload and loss
- Emphasizes simplified language, adaptive communication, dignity-preserving interactions, personalized memory anchors, and meeting people “where they are”

[Query to Insight Analytics CRAM](https://github.com/ronaldo719/CRAM-Query-to-Insight-Analytics)

- A natural-language healthcare analytics platform built entirely on **Azure**
- Lets users query a patient database using plain English (no SQL required)
- Example query: "What are the top 10 conditions among diabetic patients?"
- Produces:
  - a written summary
  - a data table
  - an auto-generated chart

## Third place ($2,500)

[ClearStep](https://github.com/joannedada/ClearStep)

- An action-first AI system intended to reduce decision overload in high-risk or confusing situations
- Architectural emphasis: **model output is not trusted**
- Uses a **validation layer** that enforces structure, corrects model errors, and blocks unsafe/misleading outputs

[DataTalk](https://github.com/JordyRHLM/DataTalk_UmsaBrainstorm)

- Ingests data from **Excel, CSV, SharePoint, and OneDrive**
- Processes it through a two-layer analytical pipeline powered by **DuckDB**
- Orchestrates four specialized AI agents:
  - understanding intent
  - reading data structure
  - generating and self-correcting SQL
  - enforcing security and auditability

[RAGulator AI Governance Engine](https://github.com/RAGulatorAPP/RAGulator)

- A governed and traceable **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** system for international trade
- Described as unifying the “Azure intelligence ecosystem” to produce grounded responses
- Includes immutable bibliographic citations
