# Mission Agent Possible: Your Developer Challenge at Microsoft Ignite 2025

Microsoft Ignite 2025 brings a unique opportunity for developers to blend creativity, AI innovation, and problem-solving in a hands-on contest: **Mission Agent Possible**.

## What Is Mission Agent Possible?

- An official contest for developers who enjoy building intelligent solutions.
- The challenge: **Create an AI agent** that solves a simulated crisis and showcases your technical skills.
- Top prizes include Xbox consoles and Microsoft Store credit (in-person attendees), plus global recognition for remote participants, such as features on the [Model Mondays podcast](https://aka.ms/model-mondays).

## Contest Details

- Contest runs from **November 18 to November 20 (PST)**.
- Full details: [https://aka.ms/ignite25/mission-agent](https://aka.ms/ignite25/mission-agent)
- Follow discussions on Discord: [https://aka.ms/ignite25/agent-contest/discord](https://aka.ms/ignite25/agent-contest/discord)
- Share your progress on social media with **#MissionAgentPossible**

## How to Choose the Right AI Model

- Model selection is key to building an effective agent.
- Guidance is provided in the [Model Selection Adventure blog](https://aka.ms/models-blog), which covers:
  - Identifying the right problem
  - Exploring model strengths and trade-offs
  - Testing outputs using GitHub Models Playground
- Resource: [https://aka.ms/models-blog](https://aka.ms/models-blog)

## Why Join?

- Get a chance to showcase technical skills to a global audience.
- Receive hands-on learning for AI agent development.
- Win prizes and gain recognition in the developer community.

## How to Prepare

- Start reviewing the model selection guide and preparing your solution using Microsoft’s AI platforms and developer resources.
- Be ready to participate as either an in-person or global contestant.
- Read eligibility rules: [Contest Official Rules](https://github.com/microsoft/ignite25-agent-contest/wiki/Microsoft-Ignite-2025-Mission%E2%80%90-Agent-Possible-Event-Contest-Official-Rules)
