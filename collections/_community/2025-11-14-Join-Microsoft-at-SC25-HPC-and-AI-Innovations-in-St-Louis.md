# Join Microsoft at SC25: HPC and AI Innovations in St. Louis

Microsoft will be present at Supercomputing 2025 (SC25) in St. Louis, MO, from November 16–21, 2025. Attendees can explore Microsoft's latest developments in high-performance computing (HPC) and artificial intelligence (AI) at Booth #1627, with a variety of technical sessions, partner events, and onsite demos.

## Booth Highlights

- **Alpine Formula 1 Showcar**: Discover how HPC drives motorsports innovation by viewing a real Alpine F1 car.
- **Silicon Wall**: Explore silicon diversity, including AMD and NVIDIA chips, alongside Microsoft’s custom silicon (Maia, Cobalt, Majorana).
- **NVIDIA Weather Modeling Demo**: See how AI and HPC predict extreme weather events using Microsoft and NVIDIA technology.
- **Coffee Bar**: Connect with technical experts over handcrafted coffee.
- **Immersive and Hardware Bars**: View live demos and next-gen AMD EPYC™/NVIDIA GB200 systems, highlighting infrastructure for demanding computational workloads.

## Conference Details

- **Dates**: November 16–21, 2025
- **Location**: St. Louis, MO
- **Expo**: Booth #1627
- **RSVP and Special Events**: Networking reception with AMD ([register here](https://aka.ms/MicrosoftAMD-Mixer)); panel luncheon with NVIDIA (fully booked)

## Earned Sessions and Technical Program

Microsoft engineers contribute to various tutorials and workshops throughout SC25:

- _Delivering HPC: Procurement, Cost Models, Metrics, Value, and More_
- _Modern High Performance I/O: Leveraging Object Stores_
- _14th International Workshop on Runtime and Operating Systems for Supercomputers_
- Papers and panels on data center congestion control, software-defined reliability, quantum chemistry with AI accelerators, and much more
- Demos and papers on technical topics including LoRA fine-tuning on Azure, agentic AI, workload intelligence, and hybrid HPC

## Partner and Booth Theater Sessions

- **Azure HPC & AI Infrastructure**: Deep dive into Azure compute and storage for HPC and AI workloads
- **Agentic AI for Science**: Explore how autonomous agents can streamline scientific research workflows
- **Hybrid HPC with Azure Arc**: Integrate on-prem clusters with Azure
- **Automating HPC Workflows with Copilot Agents**
- **Intro to LoRA Fine-Tuning on Azure**
- **Scaling the Future: NVIDIA’s GB300 NVL72 Rack for Next-Gen AI Inference**

## Additional Engagements

- Panel discussions involving Microsoft, AMD, NVIDIA, Ansys, and other ecosystem partners on the future of simulation, AI, and hybrid cloud HPC
- Networking opportunities, including the Microsoft+AMD reception at Busch Stadium

## How to Participate

Attendees can join a rich schedule of tutorials, workshops, panels, and hands-on demos. Key topics include:

- Azure-based HPC workloads
- Cloud-optimized VM families
- AI-powered digital twins
- Fine-tuning and scaling generative AI models
- Innovations in networking, storage, and hybrid architectures

## Conclusion

Microsoft’s presence at SC25 provides a valuable opportunity to engage with cutting-edge developments in Azure HPC and AI. Stop by Booth #1627 to connect with Azure experts, see partner demos, and learn about next-generation silicon and cloud technologies shaping the future of high-performance computing.
