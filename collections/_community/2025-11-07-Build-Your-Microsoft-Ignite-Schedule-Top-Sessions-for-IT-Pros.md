# Build Your Microsoft Ignite Schedule: Top Sessions for IT Pros

Welcome to the Azure Tech Community's guide for planning your experience at Microsoft Ignite 2025. With hundreds of sessions available, this roundup spotlights the most relevant content for IT professionals focusing on Azure, AI, cloud infrastructure, automation, and security.

## Recommended Sessions for IT Pros

Take advantage of curated picks to streamline your conference, maximize learning, and leave with actionable insights.

### 1. [End-to-End Migration of Applications with AI Agents to IaaS and PaaS](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/BRK140?source=sessions)

*See how Azure’s new AI-powered migration agents can simplify and accelerate every stage of your migration journey—from assessment to deployment.*

### 2. [Architecting for Resiliency on Azure Infrastructure](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/BRK178?source=sessions)

*Discover how to build resilient cloud solutions on Azure by leveraging availability zones, multi-region deployments, and fungible products. This session explores architectural patterns, platform capabilities, and best practices.*

### 3. [Migrate and Modernize Windows and SQL Server Workloads with Azure](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/LAB506-R1?source=sessions)

*Get hands-on with practical approaches to moving core enterprise workloads to Azure, improving reliability, and optimizing costs.*

### 4. [Govern Your Estate Using PowerShell and the CLI with AI](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/BRK170?source=sessions)

*Discover how to use AI with PowerShell and Azure CLI to boost automation and simplify complex commands.*

### 5. [Turning Compliance Burden into Competitive Advantage with RegScale](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/THR820?source=sessions)

*Explore how to use Microsoft’s compliance and governance frameworks to drive efficiency and differentiation.*

## Essential Ignite Moments

- **[Opening Keynote](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/KEY01?source=sessions):** Microsoft leaders unveil the latest innovations shaping the future of AI, cloud, and the developer ecosystem.
- **[Security in the Agentic Era – the Core Primitive](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/BRK1712?source=sessions):** Learn how Agentic AI will reshape security for IT operations and enterprise systems.
- **[Scale Smarter: Infrastructure for the Agentic Era](https://ignite.microsoft.com/en-US/sessions/BRK1704?source=sessions):** Updates on Azure Infrastructure—including compute, networking, hybrid management, and scalability.
