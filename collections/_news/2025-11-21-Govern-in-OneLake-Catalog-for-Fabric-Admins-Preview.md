# Govern in OneLake Catalog for Fabric Admins (Preview)

The OneLake catalog is positioned as the central hub for discovering and managing all Fabric content. It serves diverse roles — from business analysts seeking datasets to data engineers overseeing structured and unstructured data, and BI consumers searching for curated insights.

## Extending Governance Capabilities for Fabric Admins

The preview release of enhanced governance in OneLake catalog equips Fabric administrators with a unified experience to monitor, secure, and optimize their organization's data estates. Fabric admins access actionable insights and management tools directly within the catalog, ensuring they have timely information to strengthen data governance.

### Govern Tab Overview

- Immediate overview of governance status, including:
  - Number of items, workspaces, capacities, and domains
  - Distribution of items by type
  - Workspaces grouped by capacity

### Recommended Actions

- Contextual recommendations help improve governance posture, such as:
  - Setting governance policies
  - Increasing sensitivity label coverage
  - Assigning domains or tags
  - Enabling endorsements to certify and promote item discoverability
- Each recommendation includes an overview and step-by-step remediation instructions.

### In-Depth Insights

- 'View more' expands to three focused areas:
  - **Manage your data estate**: Inventory and assignment overviews, feature usage analytics
  - **Protect, secure & comply**: Sensitivity label coverage, policy adherence, data loss prevention statistics
  - **Discover, trust and reuse**: Data freshness, curation status, sharing and trust metrics
- Insights are filterable by capacity, domain, and other categories, with visualizations and drill-throughs for granular exploration.

### Copilot and Educational Resources

- Copilot integrates for interactive analytics and on-demand insights
- The 'Top solutions' section gives admins quick access to governance tools and relevant documentation.
- Links to learning resources and community engagement are prominently available.

### Ongoing Enhancements

- Microsoft continues to expand the range of governance insights and to integrate recommended actions more deeply into the admin experience. Feedback is encouraged to drive future improvements.
