# Introducing the Microsoft Defender Experts Suite: Expert-Led Integrated Security Services

Modern security teams face ever-evolving, AI-powered cyberattacks, often outpacing available talent and resources. Aarti Borkar and Andrew Conway introduce the **Microsoft Defender Experts Suite**, a comprehensive offering designed to help organizations fortify their defenses, increase resilience, and modernize security operations.

## What is the Microsoft Defender Experts Suite?

The Defender Experts Suite provides:

- **Managed Extended Detection and Response (MXDR):** With Defender Experts for XDR, organizations benefit from round-the-clock detection, investigation, and incident response, all natively integrated with Microsoft Defender. The expert team delivers proactive threat hunting and hands-on assistance to reduce alert fatigue and boost SOC efficiency.

- **Proactive and Reactive Incident Response:** With Microsoft Incident Response, organizations receive both preventative assessments (planning, simulations, readiness exercises) and rapid recovery in the event of an incident, leveraging Microsoft's deep threat intelligence and proprietary investigation tools.

- **Designated Security Advisory:** The Enhanced Designated Engineering service offers direct engagement with Microsoft security advisors, ensuring best practices, proper configuration, and operational maturity for Microsoft security technologies. This includes ongoing assessments, collaborative process modernization, and continuous improvement.

## Key Benefits

- **Defend Against Cyberthreats:** 24/7 managed XDR service, proactive threat hunting, expert incident triage and response.
- **Build Cyber Resilience:** Readiness assessments, incident response planning, and simulations to enhance the ability to prevent, withstand, and recover from cyberattacks.
- **Modernize Security Operations:** Partner with Microsoft experts to optimize processes, apply best practices, and adapt to the latest threat landscape.

## Integrated Approach

The Defender Experts Suite is designed for seamless integration, streamlining communication and reducing the complexity of managing multiple providers. With centralized expertise and coordinated workflows, security teams can act swiftly and effectively.

### Real-World Flow

1. **Onboarding:** Enhanced Designated Engineering leads deployment and configuration of Microsoft Defender workloads.
2. **Operations:** Defender Experts for XDR provides continuous monitoring and expert recommendations.
3. **Assessment:** Incident Response identifies vulnerabilities and works with advisors for remediation.
4. **Incident Management:** In the event of an attack, all components collaborate to ensure rapid recovery and adaptation.
