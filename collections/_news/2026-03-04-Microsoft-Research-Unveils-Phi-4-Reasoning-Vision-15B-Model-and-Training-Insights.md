# Microsoft Research Announces Phi-4-Reasoning-Vision-15B Model and Shares Training Best Practices

## Overview

Microsoft Research has introduced the Phi-4-reasoning-vision-15B model, reflecting new advances in large-scale multimodal artificial intelligence. This model is designed for sophisticated reasoning across multiple inputs—text, images, and audio.

## Key Highlights

- **Phi-4-reasoning-vision-15B**: A 15-billion parameter model featuring robust capabilities in understanding and reasoning from diverse media.
- **Multimodal Reinforcement Learning**: The model incorporates learning approaches that combine different modalities, enhancing its problem-solving and perceptual skills.
- **Agentic Verifier**: A mechanism within the training pipeline that acts as an autonomous verifier, improving result accuracy and reliability by evaluating outputs during training.
- **Training Best Practices**:
  - Iterative fine-tuning and scaling
  - Careful data curation for diverse media
  - Use of agentic verifiers to provide feedback loops and correct errors mid-training
  - Reinforcement learning strategies to adapt model behavior
