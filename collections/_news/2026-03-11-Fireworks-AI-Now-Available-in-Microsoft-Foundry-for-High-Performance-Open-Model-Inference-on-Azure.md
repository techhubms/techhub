# Fireworks AI Arrives on Microsoft Foundry: High-Performance, Low-Latency Open Model Inference in Azure

Microsoft has announced the public preview of Fireworks AI on Microsoft Foundry, an integration designed to make high-performance open model inference directly accessible within the Azure ecosystem. This integration is aimed at helping developers and enterprises simplify the operational lifecycle of AI models by providing a robust, unified platform.

## Key Benefits of Fireworks AI on Microsoft Foundry

- **Unified Control Plane:** Manage, evaluate, deploy, optimize, and govern open models from a single environment within Azure.
- **Enterprise-Ready AI Operations:** Move seamlessly from experimentation to production, leveraging Azure-grade governance, observability, and security.
- **Performance at Scale:** Fireworks AI delivers industry-leading inference speeds, currently processing over 13 trillion tokens daily with support for high request throughput and optimized deployments for both base and custom-weight models.
- **Flexible Deployment:** Choose between serverless, pay-per-token options with Data Zone Standard, or provisioned throughput units (PTUs) for predictable performance.
- **Bring-Your-Own-Weights (BYOW):** Easily upload and register quantized or fine-tuned model weights without restructuring your deployment stack.

## Supported Open Models

Developers now have access to several top-performing open models hosted via Fireworks AI in Foundry, including:

- DeepSeek V3.2
- OpenAI gpt-oss-120b
- Kimi K2.5
- MiniMax M2.5 (new)

These models can be used, evaluated, and deployed right inside Foundry, streamlining workflows and accelerating development.

## Getting Started: Build with Fireworks in Foundry

1. Go to [Microsoft Foundry](https://ai.azure.com) and browse the Fireworks AI open models in the model catalog.
2. Select a model to review its details.
3. Choose your deployment option (serverless or PTU) and deploy.
4. Optionally, upload your custom weight models for inference via Fireworks.

## How This Integration Supports Enterprise AI

By consolidating open model operations—evaluation, deployment, customization, monitoring, and governance—within Microsoft Foundry, organizations can:

- Accelerate time-to-value for AI projects
- Reduce infrastructure and tooling complexity
- Ensure compliance and observability in production
- Scale from experimentation to full production with confidence
