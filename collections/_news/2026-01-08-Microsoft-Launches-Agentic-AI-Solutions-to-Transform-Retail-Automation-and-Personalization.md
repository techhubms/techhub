# Microsoft Launches Agentic AI Solutions to Transform Retail Automation and Personalization

**REDMOND, Wash. — Jan. 8, 2026 —** Microsoft has unveiled a comprehensive set of agentic AI solutions for the retail industry. These innovations aim to automate and unify processes across merchandising, marketing, store operations, and fulfillment, leveraging AI to anticipate needs and deliver actionable intelligence throughout the retail value chain.

## Copilot Checkout: Seamless AI-Powered Commerce

- **Copilot Checkout** allows merchants to complete transactions directly within conversational interfaces, removing the need for shoppers to visit external sites.
- Integrates with major platforms such as Shopify, PayPal, and Stripe.
- Leading brands and online marketplaces like Urban Outfitters, Anthropologie, Ashley Furniture, and Etsy are early participants.

## Personalized Shopping Agents and Brand Agents

- **Brand Agents**: Deployable turnkey agents for Shopify merchants, engaging users with product knowledge and branded conversations.
- **Personalized Shopping Agent Template (Copilot Studio)**: Fully customizable framework enabling retailers to deliver tailored recommendations and unique digital shopping journeys across web, mobile, and physical stores.

## Catalog Enrichment Agent Template

- AI-driven template for product attribute extraction and enrichment from images and social data.
- Automates catalog management tasks such as onboarding, categorization, and error handling.
- Enhances product discovery, recommendations, and cross-channel personalization.

## Store Operations Agent Template

- Empowers store associates and managers with AI-driven insights and automated workflow orchestration.
- Offers natural language access to inventory, policies, and contextual recommendations (e.g., staffing based on sales, events, weather).
- Available as a public preview in Copilot Studio.

## Industry Vision

Microsoft believes that agentic AI will enable retailers to anticipate and adapt to change, optimize customer engagement, and empower frontline staff. These tools are intended to support strategic decision-making, automate critical workflows, and drive measurable outcomes for retailers.
