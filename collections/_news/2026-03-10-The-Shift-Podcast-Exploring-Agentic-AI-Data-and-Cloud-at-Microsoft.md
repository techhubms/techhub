# The Shift Podcast: Your Top Questions on Agentic AI

Microsoft is launching a new podcast, 'The Shift', as an evolution of 'Leading the Shift', to dive deeper into the future of agentic AI, cloud, and data platforms. Spanning eight episodes this spring, the show is driven by questions from the technology community following announcements at Microsoft Ignite.

## Podcast Focus

'Agents'—autonomous AI-driven processes—are transforming how cloud software is built and integrated. Each episode brings together engineering, product, and strategy voices across Microsoft, including teams from Azure, Foundry, and Fabric.

Key topics include:

1. Are my agents hunting for data?
2. How do agents work together?
3. Does my agent need its own database?
4. Is context engineering the new Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)?
5. What senses do agents need to act?
6. Is Postgres the wave of the future for databases?
7. Should IT teams hire agents?
8. How do we define agentic system boundaries?

## Why Listen?

- **Technical insight:** Episodes feature unscripted, practical dialogue from Microsoft product and engineering teams.
- **Hands-on focus:** Discussions center on deploying, scaling, and optimizing agent-based apps on cloud and data platforms in real environments.
- **Strategic depth:** Topics include observability, data unification, cloud scalability, security, governance, and team-driven challenges.

## Episode Sneak Peek

The first episode, "Are my agents hunting for data?", features deep dives with Microsoft Fabric and OneLake engineers on data preparation for effective agent-powered workflows. This aligns with the Microsoft Fabric Community Conference and ongoing product releases.
