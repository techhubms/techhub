# Microsoft AI Diffusion Report: Global AI Adoption Surpasses 1.2 Billion Users in Under 3 Years

Microsoft's inaugural [AI Diffusion Report](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/group/aiei/ai-diffusion/) provides a comprehensive look at the accelerating pace of artificial intelligence adoption worldwide. In less than three years, over 1.2 billion people have used AI toolsâ€”a rate of technology uptake that exceeds previous milestones, such as the spread of the internet, personal computers, or smartphones.

## Key Findings

- **Rapid AI Adoption:** More than 1.2 billion global users adopted AI tools in under three years, making AI one of the fastest-spreading general-purpose technologies.
- **Global Disparities:** While adoption is highest where connectivity and digital infrastructure are strong, nearly four billion people lack basic access to participate in the AI-driven economy.

## The Indices: Measuring AI Progress

The report introduces three analytical indices:

- **AI Frontier Index:** Tracks where AI breakthroughs and innovations are emerging globally.
- **AI Infrastructure Index:** Assesses countries' capacities to support scaling and deployment of AI systems.
- **AI Diffusion Index:** Measures the practical use and integration of AI tools in daily life, business, and society.

## Policy and Infrastructure Implications

- The report highlights the urgent need to bridge existing digital divides by improving access to digital infrastructure and fostering skills development.
- Equitable access is emphasized as critical to ensure the opportunities of AI benefit a broad population, not just those in well-connected regions.
- The findings encourage governments and organizations to invest in responsible AI policies and foundational digital infrastructure.
