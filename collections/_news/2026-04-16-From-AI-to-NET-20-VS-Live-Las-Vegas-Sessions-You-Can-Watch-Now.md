# From AI to .NET: 20 VS Live! Las Vegas Sessions You Can Watch Now

In March 2026, developers met at **VS Live! Las Vegas** for a week of technical sessions focused on where software development is heading—especially around **AI-assisted development**, **modern .NET**, **cloud-native apps**, and everyday developer productivity.

If you missed the event (or want to rewatch), Microsoft is publishing **20 recorded sessions** on the **Visual Studio YouTube channel**:

- [VS Live! Las Vegas YouTube Playlist](https://aka.ms/VSLiveLV26)

New sessions are being released at roughly **two per day**.

## What you’ll find in this series

Topics covered across the published sessions include:

- AI and Copilot-powered development
- Modern .NET and C#
- Cloud-native apps and Azure
- Developer productivity and tooling
- Real-world architecture and engineering

Speakers include Microsoft engineers working on these tools, plus industry practitioners.

The post highlights a sample of the lineup.

- Using AI in existing applications
