# Top .NET Videos and Live Streams of 2025: A Year in Review

Authored by Jon Galloway, this roundup celebrates the breadth and depth of .NET developer content published on the official .NET YouTube channel and related events throughout 2025. Whether you're a new developer or an experienced engineer, this guide highlights content for every stage of your .NET journey.

## .NET Community Growth and Engagement

- The .NET YouTube channel hit 346K subscribers, adding over 31,000 new subscribers in 2025.
- A total of 5.3 million views and 533,600 hours (over 22,000 days) of watch time.
- 169 long-form videos, 75 of which were .NET Conf sessions, and 149 live streams were published.

## Conference and Community Highlights

### .NET Conf 2025

Celebrated the release of .NET 10 with a global virtual event packed with sessions and local meetups extending into 2026.

### Recurring Series

- **.NET Unboxed / .NET Previews Unboxed**: Regular breakdowns of the latest previews and features.
- **AspiriFridays**: Live coding sessions focused on Aspire, featuring community guests.
- **Deep .NET**: Advanced dives into technical topics with Scott Hanselman and Stephen Toub ([playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLdo4fOcmZ0oX8eqDkSw4hH9cSehrGgdr1)). Notable episode: [Using AI Python Libraries in .NET Apps](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DqoxHNH9Iwo).

### .NET Community Standups

A behind-the-scenes view with core engineering teams, bringing frequent updates and direct Q&A for developers:

- [Building MCP Servers with ASP.NET Core](https://www.youtube.com/live/x_6iUhdHnhc)
- [AI-powered Blazor web apps with .NET AI template](https://www.youtube.com/live/9cwSOyavdSI)
- [Local AI on .NET using Ollama & Aspire](https://www.youtube.com/live/KX_jVyhrnWk)

## How to Access and Engage with .NET Content

- All videos available on the [official .NET YouTube channel](https://youtube.com/@dotnet) and [Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com/shows/browse?expanded=dotnet&products=dotnet).
- Regular live streams and a wide range of video resources help developers stay current, learn new skills, and interact directly with the product teams.

## Looking Forward

The .NET community continues to grow with more high-quality content on the way. Community members are invited to share their favorite sessions and wishlist topics for the coming year.
