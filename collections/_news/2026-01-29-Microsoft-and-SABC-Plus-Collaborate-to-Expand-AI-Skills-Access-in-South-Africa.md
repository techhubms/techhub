# Microsoft and SABC Plus Collaborate to Expand AI Skills Access in South Africa

Microsoft South Africa and SABC have announced a significant partnership to deliver accessible AI fluency and digital skills training to millions across South Africa, leveraging SABC Plus’s national digital platform.

### Key Highlights

- **Microsoft Elevate Initiative:** The collaboration is propelled by Microsoft Elevate and builds on the AI Skills Initiative, with an ambitious target to train one million South Africans by 2026.
- **Progress to Date:** Since launching the AI Skills Initiative, Microsoft has engaged 4 million learners and credentialed nearly half a million participants in South Africa.
- **SABC Plus Reach:** With 1.9 million registered users (25% active), SABC Plus will integrate AI fluency modules, digital literacy pathways, and co-branded credentials, making on-demand content and recognized certifications widely available.
- **Bridging Skills and Opportunity:** The initiative aims to democratize AI education and digital skills, vital for employability in the modern job market. The World Economic Forum and LinkedIn data highlight a surge in AI and data skill demand globally and regionally.

### Programme Features

- **AI Fluency Modules:** Learners can access structured AI education tailored to non-technical and technical audiences.
- **Digital Badges and Certifications:** Users completing the training can earn digital badges and Microsoft certifications, strengthening their employability.
- **Specialized Pathways:** Initiatives like Ikamva Digital, ElevateHer, Civic AI, and the YES 50K Certification Programme target students, women, non-profits, and youth, ensuring broad and inclusive involvement.
- **On-Demand Content:** Courses, assessments, and learning material are available for flexible, remote access.

### Broader Context and Impact

- **Closing the Digital Divide:** The collaboration addresses gaps in AI adoption and digital skills, especially between the Global North and South.
- **Empowering Communities:** By partnering with the country’s public broadcaster, Microsoft aims to remove barriers set by geography or background.
- **National Priorities Alignment:** The initiative supports South Africa’s goals for youth employment, digital readiness, and inclusive participation in the AI-driven economy.

### Quotations

> “By partnering with SABC, we aim to embed digital and AI skills into the daily lives of millions of South Africans. This initiative ensures that learners, educators, and job seekers can access practical, credentialed pathways that prepare them for tomorrow’s workforce.” – Tiara Pathon, Microsoft Elevate AI Skills Director, South Africa

> “South Africa’s future depends on how we equip our people with the skills to thrive in an AI-driven world. This commitment with SABC Plus is a milestone in our journey to scale access, accelerate employability, and empower communities.” – Vukani Mngxati, CEO, Microsoft South Africa

### Examples of Supporting Initiatives

- **Ikamva Digital:** Digital career pathways for students at 50 TVET colleges
- **ElevateHer:** Empowerment and AI skills for 30,000 women
- **Civic AI:** AI tools and training for 30,000 non-profit sector individuals
- **YES 50K Certification Programme:** 50,000 free certification vouchers for South African youth
