# Introducing Condensed Views for Kanban and Sprint Boards in Azure DevOps

**Author:** Dan Hellem  

One common challenge for teams using Azure DevOps Boards is handling large Kanban or Sprint boardsâ€”especially when work item cards contain many fields or tags. The new Boards hub, launched to boost readability, introduced extra spacing and padding. While this made cards clearer, it also reduced the number of items visible at a glance.

To address this, Azure DevOps now offers a *condensed view* toggle for both Kanban and Sprint boards:

- **Standard vs. Condensed:** Toggle between the regular card layout (showing full details) and a condensed option that displays only the work item title.
- **Benefits:**
  - See more cards on the board simultaneously
  - Improve backlog scanning and triage processes
  - Reduce vertical space consumption, especially for cards with multiple tags or custom fields
- **How it works:**
  - The toggle is available directly on board pages
  - Switch between full info and title-only layouts instantly, adapting to your workflow
  - Mirrors the previous condensed experience in Delivery Plans
