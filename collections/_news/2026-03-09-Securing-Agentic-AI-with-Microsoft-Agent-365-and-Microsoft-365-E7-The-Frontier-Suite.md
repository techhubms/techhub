# Securing Agentic AI for Your Frontier Transformation

**Author:** Vasu Jakkal

As the adoption of agentic AI accelerates, organizations face fresh challenges: tracking, governing, and securing AI agents at scale. Microsoft introduces two new solutions—**Microsoft Agent 365** and **Microsoft 365 E7: The Frontier Suite**—to provide end-to-end security and observability for AI agents.

## Microsoft Agent 365: Unified AI Agent Control

Agent 365 acts as a unified control plane for agentic AI, giving IT, security, and business teams visibility into all registered agents (both Microsoft-built and partner/third-party). Features include:

- **Agent Registry:** Inventory for all agents in your organization—integrated across Microsoft 365 admin center, Microsoft Defender, and Purview.
- **Behavior and Performance Observability:** Reports on usage, adoption, and risk/performance metrics worldwide.
- **Risk Evaluation:** Agent risk signals via Defender, Entra, and Purview for near real-time risk review and threat blocking.
- **Security Policy Templates:** Collaboration between IT and security with automated policy rollout (starting with Microsoft Entra).
- **Agent ID:** Unique AI agent identities managed through Microsoft Entra, supporting scalable trusted access and policy enforcement.
- **Conditional Access & Identity Governance:** Extends real-time risk-based conditional access and limited privilege management to AI agents via Microsoft Intune integration.

> *"As we are now running Agent 365 in production, Avanade has real visibility into agent activity, ... represents a critical step forward in operationalizing the agent lifecycle at scale."* —Aaron Reich, CTO, Avanade

## Data Security, Compliance, and Risk Management

Agent 365 provides:

- **Data Security Posture Management:** Insights and mitigation capabilities for data risks associated with agents.
- **Information Protection:** Agents inherit and respect Microsoft 365 sensitivity labels for consistent data handling.
- **Inline DLP for Copilot Studio Agents:** Filters sensitive data (PII, credit cards, custom types) from being processed.
- **Insider Risk and Data Lifecycle:** Insider threat protection for agent actions and configurable retention policies.
- **Audit and Communication Compliance:** Audit logs, eDiscovery, and monitoring of agent communications—treating agents as first-class audit entities.

## Defender-integrated AI Threat Protection

- **Specialized Runtime and Threat Protection:** Extends Defender to detect and mitigate advanced AI threats (prompt manipulations, model tampering, agent-based attack chains).
- **Monitoring for Foundry and Copilot Studio agents:** Detects misconfigurations, vulnerabilities, and enables incident response for agent-level threats.

## Microsoft 365 E7: The Frontier Suite

Combining intelligence and trust, E7 integrates Microsoft 365 Copilot, Agent 365, Microsoft Entra Suite, Defender, Intune, and Purview—presented as a comprehensive package for enterprise-grade AI adoption. Key highlights:

- **AI Enablement across Productivity Apps:** Extends agentic AI across email, docs, meetings, spreadsheets, and business apps.
- **Unified Security and Governance:** Equips IT and security teams with new tools for agent and user coverage at scale.
- **Availability & Pricing:** Available May 1, 2026 (Agent 365: $15/user/month, E7: $99/user/month).

## Summary

Frontier Transformation demands trust and intelligence through advanced security. Microsoft's new solutions empower organizations to observe, secure, and govern their agentic AI at the speed of innovation.
