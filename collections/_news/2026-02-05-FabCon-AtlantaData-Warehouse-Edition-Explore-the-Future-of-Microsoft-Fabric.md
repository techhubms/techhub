# FabCon Atlanta—Data Warehouse Edition: Explore the Future of Microsoft Fabric

If you're working in the Microsoft Fabric ecosystem—or curious about the state-of-the-art in cloud data warehousing—FabCon Atlanta 2026 is the must-attend event. As the largest gathering of Fabric professionals, it delivers direct insight into key platform developments, architectural decisions, and hands-on customer experiences.

## Why Attend FabCon Atlanta?

- **Roadmap Access:** Early previews and strategic sessions provide context on upcoming platform changes and why they're happening, helping professionals stay ahead and make informed architectural decisions.
- **Meet the Builders:** The Ask-the-Experts zone lets you interact directly with Fabric product managers and engineers, discuss architectures, and get practical, unfiltered answers to your toughest technical questions.
- **Customer Success Stories:** Gain visibility into how leading organizations operate Fabric Data Warehouse at scale—including their real-world solutions for performance, cost, integration, and migration.
- **Integrated Architecture:** FabCon highlights best practices in designing streamlined, end-to-end data architectures, reducing operational sprawl and complexity.

## Key Sessions

- **FabCon Atlanta Keynote:** Start with the big-picture direction for Microsoft Fabric—what's new, what's accelerating, and strategic platform investments across data, analytics, and AI.
- **The Fabric Data Warehouse Future-Proof Advantage:** Dive into the capabilities of Fabric DW, cloud-native architecture, and how it enables efficient analytics with less complexity.
- **Foundations of the Fabric Warehouse:** Understand the architectural principles and design choices shaping Fabric Data Warehouse’s evolution.
- **Modern Data Warehousing in Fabric—Cost, Scale, and Control:** Explore Custom SQL Pools for balancing workloads, ensuring performance, and managing costs without over-engineering.
- **Hardening Fabric Warehouse Security:** Learn how security is built into every layer of Fabric Data Warehouse, with new features for visibility, governance, and secure analytics at scale.
