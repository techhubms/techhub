# AI that drives change: Wayve rewrites self-driving playbook with deep learning in Azure

*Written by Chris Welsch, 8 January 2026*

Wayve, an autonomous vehicle technology company, is advancing the self-driving industry by building AI-powered solutions on Microsoft Azure. This article discusses the innovative use of deep learning models in Azure to improve self-driving systems, emphasizing the flexibility and scalability offered by Microsoft’s cloud platform.

## Key Points

- **Deep Learning and Azure:** Wayve harnesses Azure's computing resources to train and deploy deep learning models tailored to autonomous vehicles, resulting in continuous advancement of AI capabilities.
- **AI-Driven Self-Driving:** The collaboration showcases how AI and cloud services can accelerate the evolution of self-driving technology, enabling faster iteration, model training, and large-scale testing.
- **Innovation in Autonomous Technology:** By leveraging Azure, Wayve is able to process enormous datasets and utilize high-performance compute, contributing to breakthroughs in the self-driving field.
