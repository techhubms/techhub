# Aurora: Microsoft’s Open-Source AI Model for Weather and Climate Forecasting

Around the world, extreme weather events disrupt lives and economies every day, highlighting the need for accurate forecasting systems. Microsoft’s Aurora, an AI model for modeling the Earth’s systems, is engineered to address these challenges with open, collaborative innovation.

## Why Aurora Matters

- **Accurate forecasts save lives and protect infrastructure**, helping communities and industries adapt to rapidly changing weather patterns.
- Aurora leverages massive datasets to predict weather, track hurricanes, monitor air quality, and model ocean waves and energy flows.

## Commitment to Openness

- Microsoft, together with research partners like Professor Rich Turner’s lab at the University of Cambridge, has reaffirmed its goal to keep Aurora open-source and collaborative.
- The source code and model weights are open, with future releases (including training pipelines) set to be open-sourced for developer and researcher access.

## Research and Industry Collaboration

- Aurora was originally developed by Microsoft Research AI for Science, in collaboration with Professor Turner and the University of Cambridge.
- The project builds upon previous efforts, such as the SPARROW initiative, which focuses on affordable, community-deployable weather stations to expand access to high-quality environmental data.
- Partnerships with meteorological services aim to empower both the Global North and South in building and strengthening local forecasting systems tailored to specific environments.

## Technical Approach

- Aurora is trained on one of the world’s largest atmospheric datasets, enabling it to perform tasks such as:
  - Predicting wave heights
  - Tracking air quality
  - Anticipating extreme weather events
- The model can be fine-tuned with additional data for domain-specific applications.

## Cross-Industry Impact

- Innovations stemming from Aurora could benefit sectors including energy (forecasting renewable power generation), finance (anticipating weather risks), humanitarian aid (disaster response), and environmental sustainability.
- Community collaboration is encouraged, with Aurora positioned as a foundation for further research and tailored solutions.

## The Road Ahead

- Open, collaborative AI like Aurora is expected to drive global advances in climate science, disaster preparedness, and environmental data modeling.
- By providing tools for researchers and developers worldwide, Microsoft aims to foster innovation that addresses pressing planetary challenges.
