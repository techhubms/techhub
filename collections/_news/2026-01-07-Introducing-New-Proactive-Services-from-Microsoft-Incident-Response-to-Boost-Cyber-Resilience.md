# Introducing New Proactive Services from Microsoft Incident Response to Boost Cyber Resilience

Organizations face increasingly sophisticated cyberthreats that require more than just reactive measures. Microsoft Incident Response, with over a decade of experience in complex cyberattack investigation and recovery, now offers enhanced proactive services to help organizations prepare, prevent, and respond effectively to cybersecurity incidents.

## Why Cyber Resilience Matters

Building resilience is critical for organizations to prevent, withstand, and recover from cybersecurity incidents. Microsoft Incident Response leverages real-world experience to equip organizations with tools and knowledge to minimize disruption and avoid costly breaches.

## Overview of Microsoft Incident Response

- Intelligence-driven incident response from Microsoft experts
- Hands-on support before, during, and after incidents
- Access to an extensive portfolio of proactive and responsive services

[Explore Microsoft Incident Response Services](https://www.microsoft.com/security/business/microsoft-incident-response)

## Expanded Proactive Services for Enhanced Resilience

The newly introduced proactive offerings include:

- **Incident Response Plan Development**: Collaborate with Microsoft experts to create tailored response plans based on proven methodologies and real-world lessons learned.
- **Major Event Support**: Secure support and real-time monitoring during critical business events like conferences or mergers to promptly address potential threats.
- **Cyber Range Exercises**: Participate in high-fidelity simulations using Microsoft security tools to practice detection, investigation, and containment of threats, building confidence and validating response playbooks.
- **Advisory Engagements**: Receive strategic, industry-specific consulting and on-demand expert guidance to reduce risk and enhance resilience.
- **Mergers and Acquisitions Compromise Assessment**: Obtain targeted assessments to identify hidden or active compromises in acquisition or integration scenarios, ensuring secure business transitions.

## Building on Established Proactive Foundations

Microsoft's existing services remain key to building resilience:

- **Compromise Assessment**: Deep forensic investigations uncover indicators of compromise (IOCs), threat actor activity, and vulnerabilities. Includes threat hunting and clear remediation recommendations.
- **Identity Assessment & Hardening**:
    - Assessments evaluate weaknesses in authentication and access controls.
    - Identity hardening covers policy and configuration updates to guard against unauthorized access and credential-based attacks.
- **Tabletop Exercises**: Realistic incident scenarios for executive, legal, and technical teams, revealing response gaps and enhancing alignment on obligations and communication.

## Making Resilience Your Strongest Defense

Microsoft Incident Response’s proactive approach moves organizations from uncertainty to readiness. By prioritizing gap detection, defense hardening, and targeted threat insights, organizations can reduce risk, accelerate incident recovery, and strengthen their overall security posture.
