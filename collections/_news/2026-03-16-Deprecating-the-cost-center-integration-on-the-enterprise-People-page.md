Allison announces a GitHub Enterprise Cloud change: the cost center integration is being removed from the enterprise People page and the People/Licensing CSV export, with management moving to the Billing Cost centers page.<!--excerpt_end-->

## Overview

GitHub is deprecating the **cost center integration** on the **enterprise People page** and in the **People/Licensing CSV export**.

When this integration shipped in 2023, cost center data was only accessible via the API. Since then, GitHub introduced a dedicated **cost center management page** in Billing, making the People page integration redundant.

Relevant docs: [About the new billing platform for enterprises](https://docs.github.com/enterprise-cloud@latest/billing/using-the-new-billing-platform/about-the-new-billing-platform-for-enterprises)

## What is being removed

The following features are being removed from the enterprise People page:

- Cost center filter in the member filter bar
- Cost center status in member list details
- Cost center error warning banner
- Cost center section in the person detail sidebar
- Cost center column in the Licensing CSV export

## What to use instead

To manage and view cost center assignments, use the **Cost centers** page under your enterprise **billing settings**.
