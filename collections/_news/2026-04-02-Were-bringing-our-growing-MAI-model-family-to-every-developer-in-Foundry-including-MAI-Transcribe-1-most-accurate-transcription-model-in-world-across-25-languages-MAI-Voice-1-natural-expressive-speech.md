# We’re bringing our growing MAI model family to every developer in Foundry

Satya Nadella shared an update that Microsoft is bringing its **MAI model family** to developers in **Foundry**, including:

- **MAI-Transcribe-1**: described as the “most accurate transcription model in [the] world” across **25 languages**
- **MAI-Voice-1**: described as **natural, expressive speech generation**
- **MAI-Image-2**: described as Microsoft’s **most capable image model** so far
