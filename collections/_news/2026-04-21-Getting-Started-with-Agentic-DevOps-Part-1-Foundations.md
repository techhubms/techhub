## Getting started with Agentic DevOps

**Agentic DevOps** is an approach to software development where **AI-powered agents** work alongside your team across the entire software development lifecycle.

Unlike traditional AI assistance (suggestions and completions), agents can:

- Take on tasks **end-to-end**
- Collaborate across tools
- Operate across the SDLC with your guidance and approval

This series is positioned as a practical entry point, using short videos to cover key concepts and patterns, moving from fundamentals to implementation.

## Video 1: Agentic DevOps and AI-native Development

This video covers:

- How DevOps is evolving into **Agentic DevOps**
- What it means to have **AI agents** working alongside your development team
- Why this shift is happening now

Agentic DevOps extends **DevOps** and **DevSecOps** by moving from “automation” to “team augmentation”, where agents actively collaborate in development workflows.
