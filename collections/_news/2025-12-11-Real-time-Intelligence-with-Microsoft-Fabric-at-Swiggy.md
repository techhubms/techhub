# Real-time Intelligence: How India’s Swiggy Serves Millions with Microsoft Fabric

*By Chen May Yee*

Swiggy has scaled its food delivery operations to serve millions of customers across India by harnessing the power of Microsoft Fabric, a cloud-based data and analytics platform. Fabric enables Swiggy to process vast amounts of operational data in real time, supporting actionable insights and rapid decision-making.

## Key Highlights

- **Microsoft Fabric Architecture:** Swiggy utilizes Microsoft’s unified analytics platform to integrate multiple data sources and enable real-time analysis.
- **AI-driven Solutions:** Machine learning models power demand forecasting, route optimization, and personalized recommendations for users.
- **Operational Scale:** Using Fabric, Swiggy maintains high efficiency and customer responsiveness, even during peak times.
- **Data Engineering:** Advanced pipelines aggregate delivery information, transactions, and customer feedback to fine-tune business operations.
- **Impact:** The real-time intelligence delivered via Microsoft Fabric allows Swiggy to make rapid adjustments to its services, enhancing user experience and operational reliability.

## Real-world Benefits

- Improved delivery times and customer satisfaction
- Scalable analytics infrastructure for nationwide operations
- Integration of AI and ML models for continual optimization

Swiggy’s technical partnership with Microsoft illustrates the impact that cloud-based fabric and advanced data engineering can have on high-volume, time-critical platforms.
