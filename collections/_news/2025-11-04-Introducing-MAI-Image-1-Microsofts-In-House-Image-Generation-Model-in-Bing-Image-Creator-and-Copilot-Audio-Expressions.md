# Introducing MAI-Image-1: Microsoft’s In-House Image Generation Model

Microsoft has unveiled MAI-Image-1, its first image generation model developed entirely in-house, now available to creators through Bing Image Creator and Copilot Audio Expressions. This new model not only entered the top 10 in the LMArena text-to-image leaderboard but also showcases Microsoft’s ongoing commitment to building innovative, user-centric AI technologies.

## Where to Try MAI-Image-1

- **Bing Image Creator**: Find MAI-Image-1 at [bing.com/create](http://bing.com/create), in the Bing mobile app, or directly in Bing’s search bar. Users can choose MAI-Image-1 alongside DALL-E 3 and GPT-4o to fit their creative needs.
- **Copilot Audio Expressions**: Selecting Story Mode in Audio Expressions ([link](https://copilot.microsoft.com/labs/audio-expression?audiomode=story)) now uses MAI-Image-1 to visualize your stories with custom-generated images.

*Note: MAI-Image-1 is available in all countries where Bing Image Creator and Copilot Labs are accessible, except in the EU.*

## Model Highlights and Capabilities

- **Photorealistic Imagery**: Excels in rendering intricate lighting, landscapes, natural scenes, and more, performing competitively against larger, slower models.
- **Efficiency**: Offers a balance of speed and visual quality, enabling users to quickly iterate and refine their ideas.
- **Flexibility and Diversity**: Designed to avoid repetitive or generic results and deliver imagery that meets diverse creative demands.

## Development Approach

- **Purpose-Built for Creators**: MAI-Image-1 was developed with an emphasis on real-world use cases, informed by feedback from creative professionals.
- **Data Selection & Evaluation**: The team prioritized rigorous dataset curation and nuanced evaluation methodologies to ensure flexibility and authenticity in generated images.

## Microsoft’s Vision for AI

Microsoft describes this release as a step toward their mission to create AI that is supportive, helpful, and contributes positively on a global scale. The ongoing development of in-house models like MAI-Image-1 supports more immersive and useful AI-driven experiences across Microsoft’s product ecosystem.

## Getting Involved

Microsoft AI highlights its ambition and active AI research and development teams. Interested professionals are encouraged to learn more about opportunities at MAI.
