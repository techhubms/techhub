# Microsoft and Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Unite for Tech-Driven Racing Innovation

## Overview

Microsoft Corp. and the Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team have entered a multiyear partnership to bring Microsoft's cloud, AI, and developer technologies to the heart of Formula 1 racing. The collaboration is designed to optimize everything from race-day operations to factory engineering, leveraging advanced data analytics and high-performance computing.

## Partnership Details

- The partnership comes ahead of significant Formula 1 regulatory changes in 2026, which emphasize electrification, efficiency, and sustainability.
- Microsoft will provide trusted cloud and enterprise AI technologies, enabling real-time decision-making for race strategies and operations.
- More than 400 sensors in each team car produce over 1.1 million data points per second, with Azure and AI expanding high-performance simulation, analysis, and strategic modeling.

## Technological Focus Areas

- **Azure Cloud & AI**: Azure's scalable architecture supports simulation workloads, performance analysis, and team-wide analytics. Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS) allows dynamic scaling of compute resources.
- **GitHub and Microsoft 365**: GitHub accelerates software development and engineering workflows, helping integrate cutting-edge tools for modeling and simulation. Microsoft 365 supports collaboration across multiple global locations—including headquarters and trackside.
- **Intelligent Virtual Sensors**: Azure and real-time data pilots enable engineers to experiment and test quickly, minimizing delays imposed by new physical infrastructure.

## Impact on F1 Engineering and Operations

- Enables real-time intelligence for strategy, performance, and predictive analytics.
- Fosters faster collaboration, smarter development workflows, and improved operational efficiency.
- Supports compliance with the sport's regulatory and financial requirements by leveraging scalable, cloud-based platforms.

## Broader Context

- Builds on a longstanding Microsoft-Mercedes partnership across smart factory, electric vehicle, and cloud-enabled automotive solutions.
- Represents a commitment to sustainable, innovative, and inclusive motorsport for a global audience.
