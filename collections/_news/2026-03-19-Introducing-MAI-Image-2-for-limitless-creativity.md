# Introducing MAI-Image-2: for limitless creativity

*Author: stclarke (MSI team)*

![Digital painting of the ocean with deep blue waves and sunlight reflections](https://microsoft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/7afd5252efbd1b96-1.jpg)

Microsoft’s MSI team announced **MAI-Image-2**, positioning MAI as a top text-to-image lab based on the **Arena.ai** leaderboard.

- Ranked as the **#3 model family** on the Arena.ai text-to-image leaderboard: <https://arena.ai/leaderboard/text-to-image?rankBy=labs>
- Try it in the **MAI Playground**: <https://playground.microsoft.ai/chat>

## Built with creatives, for creative work

The post says MAI-Image-2 was shaped by feedback from photographers, designers, and visual storytellers, targeting “everyday creative work.”

## Enhanced photorealism

MAI-Image-2 emphasizes images that “feel like they exist in the world,” calling out:

- Natural light
- Accurate skin tones
- Lived-in environments
- Less time fixing issues in post-production

![Close-up of a person’s face with branch shadows and soft sunlight](https://microsoft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/image-2065418986.png)

![Close-up of a human iris with detailed fibers](https://microsoft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/image-2065418879.png)

The article also includes an example prompt (as plain text) and its resulting image:

- Example prompt:
  - "A glacier wall towering like a cathedral interior, deep blue ice with light refracting through layers, tiny human figure at base for scale, cinematic, cold mist in air, hyper-real detail"

![Person in red winter gear inside a massive blue ice cave](https://microsoft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/image-2065418954.png)

## Reliable in-image text generation

The post highlights **consistent text generation inside images** (e.g., posters and background signage), aiming to reduce drift between what you ask for and what appears in the image.

It specifically calls out support for producing:

- Infographics
- Slides
- Diagrams

## Rich, detailed scene generation

MAI-Image-2 is positioned for “strange,” cinematic, hyper-detailed imagery, including:

- Surreal concepts
- Ornate compositions
- Ambitious worlds

![Abstract modernist design with "MODERNISM" typography](https://microsoft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/4da3f85c5f992210-1.jpg)

![Cafe poster-style layout with event/date text](https://microsoft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/2533fbeb5b7350b0-1.png)

The article notes typographic layouts and posters can be guided through prompts covering:

- Style
- Imagery
- Fonts
- Colors

![Event poster with rider jumping a horse and typography](https://microsoft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/9e58c1e0b52b20e8-10.png)

## How to try MAI-Image-2

- Preview in **MAI Playground**: <https://playground.microsoft.ai/chat>
- The post asks users to share feedback directly with the team.

## Product rollout and developer access

The announcement states:

- MAI-Image-2 is **beginning to roll out** on:
  - **Copilot**
  - **Bing Image Creator**
- **API access** is available “today” for select Microsoft customers who need image generation at scale.
- API access is expected to open to **any developer on Microsoft Foundry** “soon.”
- Commercial use interest form (application):
