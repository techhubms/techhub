# Partner Integrations Boost Microsoft Fabric for AI and Analytics

## Overview

Microsoft Fabric continues to expand its capabilities through deep integration with partner ISV workloads. At Microsoft Ignite 2025, a host of new solutions are announced, each designed to streamline data analytics, AI strategy, and industry-specific business intelligence within the unified Microsoft Fabric and OneLake ecosystem. These integrations help organizations unlock greater value through scalable analytics, automated decisioning, and robust compliance.

## Key Partner Solutions and Integrations

### SAS Decision Builder (GA)

- Empowers organizations to rapidly design and deploy analytics-driven decision flows
- Integrates with OneLake and Fabric governance for secure, low-code automation
- Use cases: loan approvals, inventory planning, operations optimization
- [Introduction to Decision Flows (YouTube demo)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=10qQe6pUyjw)

### Esri ArcGIS Maps (Preview)

- Adds advanced geospatial analytics and mapping to Fabric workflows
- Enables spatial pattern analysis and logistics optimization
- [ArcGIS GeoAnalytics for Microsoft Fabric documentation](https://learn.microsoft.com/fabric/data-engineering/spark-arcgis-geoanalytics)

### Tonic Textual (Preview)

- Enables anonymization of sensitive data in OneLake, preserving analytic utility
- Supports compliance for AI and data science initiatives
- [Demo video: Transform sensitive text into AI-ready data](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1Crw4yqtfSM)

### 2Test (Preview)

- Provides data quality and compliance testing in OneLake
- Designed for both analysts (no code) and developers (unit tests, automation)

## Unified Data Integrations in OneLake

- Unified approach using open formats like Apache Iceberg and Delta Lake
- Enables partners to query and process data directly, reducing duplication and boosting performance

### Notable Integrations

- **Snowflake:** Native read/write access from Snowflake to Fabric; see [detailed blog post](https://aka.ms/Snowflake-Ignite25)
- **Databricks:** Planned support for direct OneLake reads and writes; [blog](https://aka.ms/Azure-Databricks-Ignite25)
- **SAP:** Zero-copy integration with business data, supporting 360° enterprise data views; [blog](https://aka.ms/SAPBDCConnect)

### Additional Partner Integrations

- **ClickHouse:** Real-time columnar analytics, direct Iceberg table querying
- **Dremio:** Self-service analytics and AI workflows; integrated via OneLake Table APIs
- **PuppyGraph:** Graph analytics for fraud, security, supply chain optimization; [Docs](https://docs.puppygraph.com/getting-started/querying-onelake-data-as-a-graph/)
- **VeloDB/Apache Doris:** Real-time and batch analytics with high concurrency and low latency; [Setup guide](https://www.velodb.io/blog/connect-apache-doris-and-microsoft-fabric-and-onelake)
- **EON:** Converts database backups into live queryable assets; supports multi-cloud backup integration
- **Telmai:** Data reliability agents continuously monitor and validate OneLake datasets; [Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jO1fnjq2Yc0)
- **DevRev:** AI knowledge graph from enterprise data in OneLake, powering business intelligence and customer operations; [Demo](https://youtu.be/f0L_4qWJHGo)
- **TiDB:** MySQL-compatible, cloud-native distributed database; supports replication to OneLake with Open Mirroring; [Learn more](https://www.pingcap.com/blog/replicate-tidb-mirrored-database-microsoft-fabric-ticdc/)

## Looking Forward

The Microsoft Fabric partner ecosystem is rapidly growing, with each integration broadening the scope of AI-powered business solutions, data reliability, robust compliance, and unified analytics. Expect ongoing innovation with vertical-specific workloads and collaborative data science initiatives supported by Fabric’s core technologies.
