# Advance your career in Data & AI with Microsoft Fabric Data Days

Fabric Data Days is a comprehensive learning series spanning over 50 days, designed for data professionals and students interested in Microsoft technologies. These events provide live and on-demand sessions covering Microsoft Fabric, Data Engineering, Data Science, and AI.

## Key Components

### Live Learning Sessions

- Real-world demos and technical deep dives
- Topics include Microsoft Fabric, Data Engineering, Data Science, and AI
- Hosted by Microsoft Data Platform MVPs, Certified Trainers, and the Fabric Community
- Recorded sessions available for flexible, on-demand learning
- Sessions support multiple languages (English, Spanish, Portuguese)

### Certification Support

- Guidance for exams DP-700, DP-600, PL-300, and DP-900
- Free and discounted certification exam vouchers
- Live study groups and skills challenges
- Practical resources to help with exam preparation

### Hands-on Contests and Challenges

- Student and professional data visualization contests
- Fabric Notebooks contest to apply hands-on learning
- Weekly QuickViz challenges to sharpen skills

### Additional Resources

- Exclusive post-session resources for all participants
- Opportunities to connect with global community and experts
