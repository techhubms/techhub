# Land O’Lakes and Microsoft Partner to Accelerate AI Innovation in Agriculture

**Overview**

Land O’Lakes Inc. and Microsoft Corp. announced a renewed multiyear alliance focused on agricultural innovation through advanced AI solutions. Building on a five-year relationship, the partnership will deliver AI-powered tools and technology to help farmers optimize yield, reduce costs, and manage risk.

## Key Initiatives

- **Co-Developed AI Tools**: Launching Oz, a digital assistant leveraging Azure AI Foundry and agricultural data to deliver real-time, customized insights to farmers and retail agronomists.
- **Digital Transformation**: Land O’Lakes has migrated over two-thirds of its IT infrastructure to Microsoft Azure and piloted Copilot licenses, enabling widespread adoption of generative AI in daily agricultural operations.
- **Custom Enterprise Copilots**: Using Copilot Tuning and proprietary data, Land O’Lakes has tailored enterprise copilots for agriculture-specific recommendations and solutions.

## Technology and Solutions

- **Oz Digital Assistant**: Provides instant responses and product guidance based on 20+ years of crop protection data, improving decision-making during the growing season.
- **Digital Ag Platform**: Enables improved soil health and modern farming practices through cloud-based analytics.
- **Digital Dairy Solution**: Employs Microsoft AI for production monitoring and supply chain optimization even in low-connectivity environments.

## Industry Impact

This alliance empowers both retail agronomists and farmers, helping them access, analyze, and act on critical farm-level data via secure, scalable Microsoft AI and cloud technologies. The collaboration exemplifies digital transformation at scale within agriculture.

## Collaboration Highlights

- **Multiyear, strategic focus on AI-powered transformation**
- **Azure as the cloud foundation for innovation**
- **Copilot-based tools tailored for agricultural use**
- **Ongoing expansion of AI-powered offerings for agriculture**

## Additional Information

- Land O’Lakes is a top agribusiness cooperative with over 100 years of history and major food and agriculture operations.
- Microsoft remains committed to democratizing AI solutions and empowering global organizations.
