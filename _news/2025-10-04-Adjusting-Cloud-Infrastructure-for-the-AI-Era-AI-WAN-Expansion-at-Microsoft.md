# Adjusting Cloud Infrastructure for the AI Era: Satya Nadella’s Insights

Satya Nadella, Chairman and CEO at Microsoft, gives an update on the company’s rapid cloud infrastructure advancements:

> "Friday evening reflection: Adjusting my cloud infra priors for the AI era… a colleague just shared the progress we are making with our AI WAN. In one project, we expanded our North American optical fiber footprint by 40% and added network capacity equal to one-fifth of our entire global network - a network that took 15 years to build now takes months!"

## Key Takeaways

- **AI-Driven Network Expansion**: Microsoft is leveraging AI to optimize and scale its wide area network (WAN), dramatically accelerating infrastructure rollout.
- **Rapid Optical Fiber Scale-Up**: North American optical fiber capacity increased by 40%, representing significant physical network expansion.
- **Network Capacity Gains**: The newly added capacity equals 20% of Microsoft’s worldwide network, previously a decade-plus effort, now condensed into months.
- **Cloud and AI Synergy**: AI technologies are being embedded into core infrastructure, streamlining deployments and reshaping network economics and operational agility.
