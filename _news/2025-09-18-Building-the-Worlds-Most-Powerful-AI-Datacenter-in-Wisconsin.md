# Building the World's Most Powerful AI Datacenter in Wisconsin

Microsoft is in the final construction phases of Fairwater, a cutting-edge AI datacenter located in Mount Pleasant, Wisconsin. Set to go online in early 2026, the facility is described as the world's most powerful AI datacenter, representing a total planned investment of over $7 billion.

## Technical Capabilities

- **GPU Clusters:** The datacenter will house hundreds of thousands of high-end NVIDIA GPUs arranged in seamless clusters, delivering up to ten times the performance of today's fastest supercomputers.
- **AI Training:** It's designed for training frontier AI models, providing the backbone for breakthroughs in fields like medicine, science, and advanced engineering.
- **Data Infrastructure:** The site features enough fiber optic cable to encircle the planet four times.

## Sustainability by Design

- **Cooling:** More than 90% of the facility uses a closed-loop liquid cooling system, dramatically reducing water consumption. On hotter days, air-based cooling is used, minimizing environmental impact.
- **Energy:** Microsoft is pre-paying for the datacenter's electrical infrastructure to avoid driving up local energy costs. All fossil-based energy consumed will be matched one-to-one with renewable energy—supported by a new 250 MW solar project in Portage County.
- **Environmental Partnerships:** Collaborations with local organizations, such as the Root-Pike Watershed Initiative Network, support multiple ecological restoration projects in the region.

## Economic and Social Impact

- **Job Creation:** Over 3,000 construction workers were employed at peak activity, with permanent staff expected to reach 800 after full build-out.
- **Education:** The Datacenter Academy in partnership with Gateway Technical College aims to train more than 1,000 students in five years for datacenter and IT roles.
- **AI Innovation:** A manufacturing-focused AI Co-Innovation Lab at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee has already helped regional companies turn AI ideas into real-world solutions.
- **Broadband Expansion:** The initiative also addresses digital equity by bringing high-speed internet to thousands of rural and suburban residents.

## Community Commitment

Microsoft positions the Mount Pleasant datacenter as more than just a technical marvel; it's a blueprint for responsible, inclusive innovation—demonstrating how massive AI infrastructure can empower both industry and community.
