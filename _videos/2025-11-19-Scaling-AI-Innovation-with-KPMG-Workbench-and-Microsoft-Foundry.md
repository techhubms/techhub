# Scaling AI Innovation with KPMG Workbench and Microsoft Foundry

This Microsoft Ignite 2025 session features Tim Denley and Microsoft Events, highlighting KPMG's journey in building the Workbench platform—an enterprise-scale AI solution developed with Microsoft.

## Overview

- **KPMG Workbench** is a global, scalable platform managing over 50 specialized AI agents, with architecture supporting thousands.
- Built using **Microsoft Foundry**, the platform emphasizes security, compliance, and interoperability, crucial for tax, audit, and advisory firms.
- Integrates KPMG's deep industry expertise into digital interactions, improving service delivery and client engagement.

## Key Features & Capabilities

- **Agent Management:** Workbench coordinates numerous AI agents, allowing scalable and flexible responses to complex scenarios.
- **Shared Design Language:** Ensures usability and accessibility for KPMG's 275,000 professionals worldwide.
- **Casecraft Module:** Processes input files across nine content categories, providing quality checks for voice, style, and compliance.

## Value Proposition

- Streamlines enterprise processes in sensitive industries by automating routine tasks while maintaining regulatory compliance.
- Enables secure, compliant, and interoperable operations across global teams.
- Supports rapid digital transformation by embedding firm expertise into AI-driven workflows.
