# Agentic AI Agents: Transforming Retail with Microsoft Azure

**Presented at Microsoft Ignite 2025 – BRK382**

## Overview

Agentic AI is revolutionizing retail operations, from personalized shopping experiences to catalog enrichment and employee empowerment. This session guides retail technologists through:

- The concept of agentic AI for retail
- Case study: Personalized Shopping Agent (Ralph Lauren example)
- Connecting agents to data environments
- Demo options for agent development
- Publishing agents to user experience channels
- Facilitating consumer interactions via question-based recommendations
- Implementation strategies for scalability and simplicity
- Step-by-step walkthrough: Catalog Enrichment Agent workflow
- Empowering frontline workers with AI and orchestrating multiple agents
- Strategic advice: Customer-centric channels and unified engagement
- Marketplace availability: Partner-built agent solutions and demo exploration
