# Best Practices for Always-On AI Agents Using Copilot Studio and Power Platform

**Speaker:** Shivani Pradhan  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025

## Overview

This session focuses on best practices to architect and deploy always-on AI agents that support mission-critical workloads, leveraging Microsoft services like Copilot Studio, Power Platform, and Dataverse.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Power Platform and Azure Outage**: Lessons on platform resilience and the business risks associated with major cloud outages, with a real-world case from the Norway Azure event.
- **Continuous Operations**: Strategies to ensure uninterrupted workflow execution in frontline and executive scenarios.
- **Copilot Studio in Insurance**: Exploring how Copilot Studio enables resilient agent environments, including a look at agent architecture specific to the insurance industry.
- **Failover and Data Loss**: Explanation of typical failover processes, caution about the one-minute data loss during transitions, and emphasizing responsibility before triggering failover.
- **Resilient Deployment**: Introduction to deployment methodologies that improve safety and rapid recovery.
- **Monitoring and Observability**: Best practices for real-time monitoring, scaling, and automation to ensure agents are robust against failure.
- **API Automation and SSDR**: Automating secondary site disaster recovery (SSDR) through APIs while stressing the importance of human oversight for critical processes.
- **Compliance Drills**: Using simulated production failovers to train teams and validate preparedness for real disruptions.
