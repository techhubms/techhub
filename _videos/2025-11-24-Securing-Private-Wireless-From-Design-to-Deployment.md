# Securing Private Wireless: From Design to Deployment

## Session Overview

Presented by Krishnan Jagannathan and Rick Lievano at Microsoft Ignite 2025, this session details proven frameworks for securing private wireless (including 5G) networks. The content shares how Azure-based AI platforms and integrated defense strategies have resulted in a significant drop in security incidents, cost savings, and protection for critical telecom infrastructure.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Network Security Domains:** Introduces network security for telecom, focusing on distributed architectures and the challenges therein.
- **Operational Efficiency & Security:** Balancing operational effectiveness with heightened security demands in telco environments.
- **Credential Theft Protection:** Examines increasing risks of credential theft and the need for more robust authentication mechanisms across wireless networks.
- **Azure-Based AI Security Platform:** Explains how Azure powers advanced AI capabilities to support telecom security operations, from threat detection to automated responses.
- **Incident Response:** Highlights the role playbooks and real-world use cases play in effective cyber incident management.
- **Telecom Collaboration & Identity Management:** Shares insights on collaborative strategies among providers for better identity management and defense against evolving threats.
- **AI-Powered Threat Hunting:** Demonstrates how AI-driven tools use intelligence models, such as the CIA diamond model, to proactively identify and mitigate complex threats.
- **5G Security Orchestration:** Introduces orchestration solutions for securing 5G deployments, including a demo preview.
- **Q&A and Closing Remarks:** Wraps up with an open forum addressing audience questions on network security operations and best practices.
