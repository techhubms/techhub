# Partners and Agentic AI: Transforming Energy and Resources

**Session from Microsoft Ignite 2025**

Speakers: Stephanie Comardelle, Mike Rossi, Rob Schapiro

## Overview

This intermediate breakout session examines how energy and resource companies are leveraging AI and agent-based technologies to drive operational efficiency, resilience, and innovation. The speakers highlight Microsoft and partner initiatives that accelerate AI transformation and outline paths toward becoming a 'Frontier Firm' in the industry.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Technology Adoption Curves**: Analysis of how energy firms progress through digital transformation stages.
- **Frontier Firms Introduction**: Insights from Microsoft Work Trends Index, featuring industry examples like Chevron and Petronas.
- **Industrial Practice at Microsoft Partners**: Overview of partner-driven solutions enabling AI deployment at scale.
- **Human & Digital Intelligence**: Strategies for orchestrating the workforce and digital agents to enable real-time decisions using accessible data.
- **Governance, Security, and Agent 365**: Discussion of ensuring secure AI adoption through governance models and Microsoft’s Agent 365 announcement.
- **Future Outlook**: Preparing the next generation of energy leaders for a data-driven future.
- **Key Takeaway**: AI adoption empowers practitioners rather than threatening jobs, with an emphasis on upskilling for success.
