# Unified Digital Resilience: Integrating Cisco and Microsoft Security on Azure

**Speakers:** Richard Diver, Anthony Pierce  
**Event:** Microsoft Ignite 2025 – Session BRKSP483  
**Level:** Intermediate (200)

## Session Overview

This session explores the partnership between Cisco and Microsoft and their joint effort to deliver unified digital resilience via Microsoft Azure. Key topics include:

- Integrating visibility, security, automation, and AI-powered insights
- Transforming security operations through unified threat detection, investigation, and response
- Strategies to accelerate threat detection, improve operational efficiency, and support business continuity

## Key Chapters

### 0:00 – Introduction and Welcome

Overview of session goals and the collaboration between Microsoft and Cisco.

### 1:26 – Early Collaboration Origins

How the partnership began at an oil company, leveraging both Microsoft and Cisco tools for security and resource management.

### 3:10 – Leveraging Resources Effectively

Practical strategies for maximizing the capabilities of Microsoft and Cisco tools in enterprise environments.

### 8:06 – Threat Modeling Introduction

Explaining the fundamentals of threat modeling and understanding modern attack vectors in hybrid cloud settings.

### 8:37 – Building a Security Matrix

Discussion of prevention, detection, and response techniques within joint solutions.

### 17:29 – Threat Modeling and AI

Applying security principles to AI development and deployment, addressing unique risks and safeguards.

### 18:25 – AI Access Privilege: White-Hat Hacker Example

Illustrative story highlighting the importance of securing AI access privileges against exploitation.

### 26:02 – Evolving Threat Landscape

Insights into proactive collaboration's role in adapting to rapidly evolving cybersecurity challenges.

### 29:53 – Optimizing Existing Tools

How organizations can maximize their existing security investments for efficiency and cost savings.
