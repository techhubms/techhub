# Overview of Managed Solutions and Secure Operations in Power Platform

This Microsoft Ignite 2025 session, presented by Ryan Jones, is designed for IT leaders and platform owners seeking to operate confidently in an AI-driven world using Microsoft Power Platform.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Data Protection & Compliance**: Discussion on tools and strategies for protecting data and ensuring compliance within Power Platform solutions.
- **Secure Development**: Best practices for governing solution development and enforcing security for enterprise apps and agents.
- **Copilot Studio**: Demonstrations on how Copilot Studio empowers enterprises to build, configure, and monitor AI agents with extensive security and governance controls.
- **Operational Monitoring**: Configuration of Copilot Hub monitoring, with a focus on operational health, issue monitoring, and alerting for apps and workflows.
- **Managed Solutions**: Lessons on application lifecycle management (ALM) using default pipelines, integrating source control, and streamlined testing for AI-driven agents.
- **Scalable Governance**: Frameworks for managing groups, applying rules, and exposing monitoring tools to makers within Power Platform.

## Real-World Industry Examples

- **Lumen Technologies & Heineken**: Highlighted use cases demonstrating Microsoft’s frameworks for building compliant, secure solutions across industry verticals.

## Development Focus

- **Accelerating Line-of-Business Apps**: Guidance on leveraging Power Platform as the fastest method for building enterprise-grade, line-of-business applications for both professional developers and makers.
