### Introduction to Frontier Firms and Modern Cloud Foundations

Learn how leading enterprises are reshaping their operations using Azure's modern cloud infrastructure. The session begins by exploring pivotal shifts toward cloud-driven business transformation.

### Key Technical Challenges

- **Technical Debt**: Identifying and addressing legacy system constraints
- **Grunt Work**: Streamlining repetitive tasks through automation
- **Skill Gaps**: Practical approaches to upskill and reskill teams for cloud-first strategies

### Overcoming Migration Barriers

- Leveraging automation tools and human-agent collaboration to simplify and accelerate migration
- Broad applicability of agentic capabilities across workloads

### AI Transformation and Data Foundations

- Building data-centric architectures as a foundation for AI adoption in Azure environments
- Understanding how migration and modernization interface with AI strategies for long-term value

### VMware Environment Modernization

- Best practices for migrating and modernizing workloads from VMware to Azure

### Security & Compliance

- Security considerations for cloud migration, including regulatory compliance, risk management, and Microsoft security tooling

### Connecting IT and Business Teams

- Techniques for aligning technical functions with business objectives, fostering collaborative delivery of solutions

### Real-World Modernization Goals

- Case studies and practical examples, such as modernizing a 25-year-old bespoke system
