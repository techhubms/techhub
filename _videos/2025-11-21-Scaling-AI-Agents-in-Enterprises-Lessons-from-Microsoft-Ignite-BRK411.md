# Scaling AI Agents in Enterprises: Lessons from Microsoft Ignite BRK411

## Overview

This session, delivered as a silent stage breakout at Microsoft Ignite 2025 and featuring Nick Baguley, Tom Davis, Zion Zatlavi, and Sharon Zhang, provides an inside look at scaling AI agentic systems in complex enterprise environments.

## Key Discussion Chapters

- **Framing the Discussion:** Introduction to Azure AI Foundry and the agentic stack as a future architecture.
- **Enterprise Adoption Model:** The agentic layer is identified as a central component for scalable, future-proof enterprise AI governance.
- **Data Challenges in Finance:** Strategies to overcome data silos and improve access when scaling agents in financial services.
- **Automation & Compliance:** Achieving large-scale automation and compliance improvements through agent-driven architectures.
- **Empowering Users:** Examples of enabling business users to create and customize their own AI assistants for domain-specific tasks.
- **Case Study:** An ideal enterprise AI implementation illustrated with a sports brand, emphasizing stepwise scaling and customization.
- **Expert Teams & Data Accuracy:** Insights into the importance of small expert teams and maintaining data accuracy to drive rapid scaling.
- **Human-Centric Transformation:** As AI agents automate routine work, enterprise teams can shift their focus to higher-value strategic efforts.
- **Democratization of AI:** Conversation highlights how agentic systems allow users to delegate tasks they dislike or lack proficiency in to intelligent agents.

## Practical Takeaways

- **Copilot Readiness:** Being ‘Copilot ready’ involves robust data foundations, governance, and trust frameworks.
- **Agentic Stack Design:** Future architectures require layer-based models for security, compliance, and customization.
- **Security & Governance:** Agent solutions must ensure data accuracy, enforce compliance, and foster user trust.
- **Innovative Solutions:** Demonstrated value includes automation, business process improvements, and better user experiences.
